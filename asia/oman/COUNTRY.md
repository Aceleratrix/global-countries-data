# Oman 🇴🇲
Oman, officially the Sultanate of Oman, is a country on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula in Western Asia. It has a strategic location on the Arabian Sea and the Gulf of Oman.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Muscat (population ~1.7 million)
- **Population**: ~5.3 million (2024 est)
- **GDP**: $0.15 trillion (73rd largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Omani Rial (OMR)
- **Official Language**: Arabic
- **Area**: 309,500 km²
- **HDI**: 0.813 (Very High, 54th globally)

## Economy
Oman's economy is dominated by oil and gas exports, which account for over 60% of government revenue and 70% of export earnings. Recent economic diversification efforts focus on tourism, logistics, and mining.

## Geography
Oman borders Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, and Yemen. It has a diverse landscape including mountains, deserts, and coastlines. The highest point is Jabal Shams (3,009 m).

## Government
Oman is an absolute monarchy led by Sultan Haitham bin Tariq. It gained independence from Portugal in 1650. The current constitution was adopted in 1996 and amended in 2011.

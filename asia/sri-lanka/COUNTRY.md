# Sri Lanka 🇱🇰
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka
- **Capital**: Colombo (Commercial), Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte (Administrative)
- **Population**: ~22.2 million (2023 estimate)
- **GDP**: $0.14 trillion (77th largest economy globally, 2023)
- **GDP per capita**: $6,300 (2023)
- **Currency**: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR)
- **Languages**: Sinhala (official), Tamil (official), English (link language)
- **Independence**: February 4, 1948 (from the United Kingdom)
- **Government**: Semi-presidential representative democratic republic

## Key Facts
- Buddhism is the majority religion (~70.2% of population)
- Literacy rate: ~92.3% (2023)
- Life expectancy: ~77.2 years (2023)
- Major exports: Textiles, tea, rubber, spices, gems
- Famous for: Ceylon tea, ancient Buddhist temples, wildlife (leopards, elephants), cricket

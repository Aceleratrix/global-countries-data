# 🇮🇩 Indonesia

**Capital:** Jakarta  
**Population:** 281.2 million (2024) – 4th most populous country  
**Currency:** Indonesian Rupiah (IDR, Rp)  
**Official Language:** Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia)  
**Government:** Presidential Republic  
**GDP (Nominal):** $1.3 trillion (2024) – 16th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $4,580  
**HDI:** 0.705 (High) – 114th globally  
**Time Zone:** UTC+7 to UTC+9 (3 time zones)  

## 🌏 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** ID (Alpha-2), IDN (Alpha-3), 360 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .id
- **Calling Code:** +62
- **ASEAN Member:** Yes (largest economy in ASEAN)
- **G20 Member:** Yes
- **Largest Archipelago:** 17,000+ islands

## 🏢 Business Environment

Indonesia is Southeast Asia's largest economy with massive digital transformation underway. Key sectors include palm oil, mining (nickel, coal), textiles, automotive, and rapidly growing digital economy.

**Key Industries:**
- Palm Oil (world's largest producer)
- Nickel Mining (EV battery supply chain)
- Textiles & Garments
- Automotive (2nd largest in ASEAN)
- Digital Economy ($77B by 2025)
- Tourism (Bali, Komodo, Raja Ampat)
- Coal Mining

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Indonesia launched its **National AI Strategy 2020-2045**:
- **1M+ tech talent** by 2030
- **GDP contribution target:** $366B from AI by 2030
- Tech hubs: Jakarta, Bandung, Yogyakarta, Bali

**Major Tech/AI Companies:**
- **GoTo Group** – Gojek + Tokopedia merger ($23B peak valuation)
- **Sea Group (Shopee Indonesia)** – E-commerce & fintech
- **Bukalapak** – E-commerce platform
- **Akulaku** – AI-driven fintech lending
- **Pinhome** – PropTech with AI

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 5.32% (2024)
- **Inflation Rate:** 2.8% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 22%
- **Minimum Wage (Jakarta):** IDR 4,900,000 (~US$327/month)
- **GDP Growth:** 5.0% (2024)

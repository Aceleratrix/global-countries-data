# Lebanon

## Basic Information
- Official Name: Republic of Lebanon
- Capital: Beirut
- Population: ~5,817,100 (2023 estimate)
- Area: 10,452 km²
- Official Languages: Arabic, French (recognized)
- Currency: Lebanese Pound (LBP)
- Time Zone: UTC+2 (Eastern European Time), UTC+3 (Eastern European Summer Time)
- Calling Code: +961
- ISO Codes: LB (alpha-2), LBN (alpha-3), 422 (numeric)
- Borders: Syria (north, east), Israel (south)
- Coastline: 225 km along the Mediterranean Sea (west)

## Quick Facts
- 5 UNESCO World Heritage Sites
- Home to the oldest continuously inhabited city (Byblos)
- Parliamentary republic with a power-sharing political system

# Timor-Leste (East Timor) 🇹🇱

**Official Name**: Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste  
**Common Name**: East Timor / Timor-Leste  
**Capital**: Dili  
**Area**: 14,874 km²  
**Population**: ~1,318,445 (2023 estimate)  
**Languages**: Tetum (official), Portuguese (official), Indonesian, English  
**Currency**: US Dollar (USD)  
**Calling Code**: +670  
**ISO Codes**: TL (alpha-2), TLS (alpha-3), 626 (numeric)  
**Government**: Unitary semi-presidential republic  
**Independence**: May 20, 2002 (from Indonesia/UN administration)  

## Quick Facts
- Asia's youngest nation, gaining independence in 2002
- First country to join ASEAN as a full member in 2023
- Heavily reliant on oil and gas exports for revenue
- 96% of population identifies as Catholic

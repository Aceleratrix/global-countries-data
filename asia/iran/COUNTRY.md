# Iran (Islamic Republic of Iran) 🇮🇷

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Islamic Republic of Iran
- **Capital**: Tehran (Population: ~9 million)
- **Largest City**: Tehran
- **Population**: ~88 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 1,648,195 km² (17th largest country globally)
- **GDP**: $0.44 trillion (30th largest economy worldwide, 2023 IMF estimate)
- **Currency**: Iranian Rial (IRR)
- **Official Language**: Persian (Farsi)
- **Religion**: Islam (Shia Islam is the state religion; ~90-95% Shia, ~5-10% Sunni)
- **Government Type**: Islamic Republic (theocratic-presidential hybrid system)
- **Current Supreme Leader**: Ali Khamenei
- **Current President**: Masoud Pezeshkian (elected 2024)

## Key Facts
- **Major Natural Resources**: 4th largest crude oil reserves, 2nd largest natural gas reserves globally
- **Major Exports**: Crude oil, natural gas, petrochemicals, handmade carpets, pistachios, saffron
- **Major Trading Partners**: China, India, Turkey, United Arab Emirates
- **Sanctions**: Subject to extensive US/EU economic sanctions since 1979, restricting energy exports, financial transactions, and technology imports
- **Historical Note**: Home to one of the world's oldest continuous civilizations (ancient Persian Empire), site of the 1979 Islamic Revolution, signatory to the 2015 JCPOA nuclear agreement

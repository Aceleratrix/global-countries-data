# Iraq (🇮🇶)
## Republic of Iraq (Jumhūriyyat al-ʿIrāq)
### Basic Information
- **Capital**: Baghdad
- **Area**: 438,317 km²
- **Population**: 43,533,592 (2023 UN estimate)
- **Currency**: Iraqi dinar (IQD)
- **Official Languages**: Arabic, Kurdish (official in Kurdistan Region)
- **Religion**: Islam (95-98% Muslim: Shia ~60-65%, Sunni ~32-37%; small Christian/Jewish/Zoroastrian minorities)
- **Government Type**: Federal parliamentary republic
- **Independence**: October 3, 1932 (from British Mandate of Mesopotamia)
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $264.18 billion (2023 World Bank)
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$6,076 (2023)
- **Calling Code**: +964
- **Internet TLD**: .iq

### Key Facts
- Located in Western Asia, bordered by Turkey, Iran, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Syria, and the Persian Gulf
- World's fifth-largest proven oil reserves (per OPEC 2024)
- Home to ancient Mesopotamian civilizations (Sumer, Akkad, Babylon, Assyria)
- Member of OPEC, Arab League, United Nations, Organization of Islamic Cooperation

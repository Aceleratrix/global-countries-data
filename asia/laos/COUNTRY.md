# Laos (Lao People's Democratic Republic) 🇱🇦
## Overview
Laos, officially the Lao People's Democratic Republic (Lao PDR), is a landlocked country in Southeast Asia, bordered by China to the north, Vietnam to the east, Cambodia to the southeast, Thailand to the west, and Myanmar to the northwest. It is the only landlocked country in Southeast Asia.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Vientiane
- **Population**: ~7.5 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 237,955 km²
- **Currency**: Lao kip (LAK, ₭)
- **Official Language**: Lao
- **Government**: Unitary Marxist-Leninist one-party socialist republic
- **Current President**: Thongloun Sisoulith
- **Current Prime Minister**: Sonexay Siphandone
- **Independence**: 19 July 1949 (from France)
- **Dominant Religion**: Theravada Buddhism (~67% of population)

# Pakistan 🇵🇰
## Overview
Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country with a population exceeding 240 million people. It shares land borders with India to the east, Afghanistan to the west, Iran to the southwest, and China to the northeast. It has a coastline along the Arabian Sea to the south.

## Key Facts
- Capital: Islamabad
- Largest city: Karachi
- Official languages: Urdu, English
- Currency: Pakistani rupee (PKR)
- Government: Federal parliamentary constitutional republic
- Area: 881,913 km²
- Population (2023): ~241.5 million
- GDP (nominal, 2024): ~$374 billion
- HDI (2022): 0.540 (medium)

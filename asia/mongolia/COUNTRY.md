# Mongolia 🇲🇳
## Overview
Mongolia is a landlocked country in East Asia, bordered by Russia to the north and China to the south. It is the world's largest landlocked country that does not border a closed sea, and the most sparsely populated sovereign state, with a population of approximately 3.4 million people as of 2024. The capital and largest city is Ulaanbaatar.

# South Korea (Republic of Korea)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Korea (ROK)
- **Common Name**: South Korea
- **Capital**: Seoul
- **Largest City**: Seoul
- **Area**: 100,363 km² (38,750 sq mi)
- **Population (2023)**: 51,784,059
- **Currency**: South Korean won (KRW, ₩)
- **Languages**: Korean (official)
- **Time Zone**: Korea Standard Time (UTC+9)
- **Driving Side**: Right
- **Calling Code**: +82
- **ISO 3166 Code**: KR

## Key Facts
- G20 member, OECD member, UN member
- Major economies: Electronics, automotive, shipbuilding, steel, petrochemicals
- Home to global brands like Samsung, Hyundai, LG, Kia
- Known for K-pop, Korean cinema (Parasite, Squid Game), and Korean cuisine (kimchi, bibimbap)

# 🇰🇷 South Korea

**Capital:** Seoul  
**Population:** 51.7 million (2024)  
**Currency:** South Korean Won (KRW)  
**Official Language:** Korean (Hangul)  
**Government:** Presidential Republic  
**GDP (Nominal):** $1.7 trillion (2024) – 12th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $33,120  
**HDI:** 0.925 (Very High) – 19th globally  
**Time Zone:** KST (UTC+9)  

## 🌏 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** KR (Alpha-2), KOR (Alpha-3), 410 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .kr
- **Calling Code:** +82
- **OECD Member:** Yes (1996)
- **G20 Member:** Yes
- **Demilitarized Zone (DMZ):** Border with North Korea

## 🏢 Business Environment

South Korea is a global technology powerhouse with world-leading companies in electronics, semiconductors, automotive, and entertainment (K-pop).

**Key Industries:**
- Semiconductors (Samsung, SK Hynix – 60% global market share)
- Electronics (Samsung, LG – smartphones, displays, appliances)
- Automotive (Hyundai, Kia – 5th largest producer)
- Shipbuilding ( world's largest shipbuilder)
- Entertainment (K-pop, K-drama – global cultural export)
- Telecommunications (5G leader, 95% penetration)

## 🤖 AI Readiness

South Korea is one of the world's most AI-ready nations with the **Korean AI Strategy**:
- $176 billion national AI investment (2024-2027)
- World's fastest internet (average 200+ Mbps)
- 95% 5G coverage (world's first nationwide 5G network)
- Home to Samsung AI, Naver AI, KakaoBrain

**Major AI Companies/Initiatives:**
- **Naver Clova** (Korea's leading AI assistant)
- **KakaoBrain** (KoGPT, Karlo image generation)
- **Samsung AI** (AI in consumer electronics)
- **LG AI Research** (Vision AI, language models)
- **Upstage** (Solar LLM, Korean-focused)

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 2.7% (2024, near historical low)
- **Inflation Rate:** 2.8% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 24% (large companies), 10% (SMEs)
- **Minimum Wage:** ₩9,860/hour (2024, ~$7.40 USD)
- **GDP Growth:** 2.3% (2024)

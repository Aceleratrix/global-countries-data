# Brunei Darussalam
**Capital:** Bandar Seri Begawan
**Population:** 445,000 (2023 estimate)
**GDP:** $15.2 billion (2023, oil and gas dependent economy)
**Official Languages:** Malay
**Currency:** Brunei Dollar (BND, pegged 1:1 to SGD)
**Region:** Southeast Asia (Borneo Island)
**Government:** Unitary Islamic absolute monarchy (Sultanate)
**Area:** 5,765 km²
**Time Zone:** UTC+8 (Brunei Time)
**Calling Code:** +673
**Internet TLD:** .bn

## Key Facts
- Major oil and gas exporter, OPEC member since 2006
- High GDP per capita (~$35,000, 2023)
- Islamic Sharia law applied alongside civil law since 2014
- No personal income tax, free healthcare and education for citizens
- Literacy rate ~98%, life expectancy ~76 years
- Majority Malay (66%), followed by Chinese (10%), indigenous groups (24%)

# Turkmenistan (Türkmenistan) 🇹🇲
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Turkmenistan (Türkmenistan)
- **Capital**: Ashgabat
- **Largest City**: Ashgabat
- **Population (2023)**: ~6,202,000
- **Area**: 491,210 km² (189,657 sq mi)
- **Official Language**: Turkmen
- **Widely Spoken Languages**: Russian, Uzbek, Kazakh
- **Currency**: Turkmenistani manat (TMT)
- **Calling Code**: +993
- **Internet TLD**: .tm
- **Independence**: October 27, 1991 (from the Soviet Union)
- **Current President**: Serdar Berdimuhamedow
- **System**: Presidential republic (no prime minister position)

## Key Facts
- Landlocked Central Asian country bordered by Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Afghanistan, Iran, and the Caspian Sea.
- Holds the world's 4th-largest natural gas reserves.
- Darvaza Gas Crater ("Door to Hell") burns continuously since 1971.
- Declared permanent UN neutrality in 1995.
- Turkmen carpet weaving and Ak halteke horse breeding are UNESCO-recognized national symbols.

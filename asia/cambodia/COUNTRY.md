# Cambodia (Kingdom of Cambodia) 🇰🇭
## Basic Info
- Capital: Phnom Penh
- Population: 17,000,000 (2023 estimate)
- Area: 181,035 km²
- GDP: $0.20T (2023 nominal, 63rd largest economy globally)
- Currency: Cambodian riel (KHR), US dollar (USD) widely used
- Official Language: Khmer
- Government: Unitary parliamentary constitutional monarchy
- Head of State: King Norodom Sihamoni
- Head of Government: Prime Minister Hun Manet
- Independence: November 9, 1953 (from France)
- Time Zone: UTC+7
- Official Religion: Theravada Buddhism (~97% of population)

## Key Facts
- Borders: Thailand (west/northwest), Laos (north), Vietnam (east)
- Major Cities: Phnom Penh (2,000,000), Siem Reap (250,000), Battambang (200,000)
- Main Economic Sectors: Agriculture, tourism, textiles, manufacturing, construction
- Top Exports: Clothing, footwear, rice, rubber, bicycles
- UN Member: 1955
- ASEAN Member: 1999

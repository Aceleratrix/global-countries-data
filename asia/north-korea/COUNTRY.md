# North Korea (Democratic People's Republic of Korea)
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK)
- **Capital**: Pyongyang
- **Population**: ~25.8 million (2023 estimate, UN)
- **Area**: 120,540 km²
- **Languages**: Korean (official)
- **Currency**: North Korean won (KPW)
- **ISO Codes**: Alpha-2: KP, Alpha-3: PRK, Numeric: 408
- **Calling Code**: +850
- **Government Type**: Juche-oriented socialist republic, dynastic totalitarian regime under the Kim family
- **Head of State**: Kim Jong Un (General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Korea, State Affairs Commission Chairman)
- **Neighboring Countries**: China (northwest), Russia (northeast), South Korea (south, across the Korean Demilitarized Zone)
- **Time Zone**: Pyongyang Time (UTC+9)

## Key Facts
- Founding Date: September 9, 1948
- Major Religion: Traditionally Buddhism and Confucianism, but state promotes secular Juche ideology
- GDP (PPP): ~$40 billion (2023 estimate, CIA World Factbook)
- Main Industries: Military equipment, mining (coal, iron ore), agriculture, textiles
- Literacy Rate: ~99% (self-reported, 2022)
- Life Expectancy: 72.3 years (2023 estimate, UN)

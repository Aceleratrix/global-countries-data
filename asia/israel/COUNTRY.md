# Israel (🇮🇱)
## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Jerusalem (de jure); Tel Aviv-Yafo (financial/diplomatic center)
- **Population**: 9.4 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 20,770 km²
- **GDP**: $0.39 trillion (2023 nominal)
- **Global GDP Rank**: 33rd
- **GDP per capita**: $41,000 (2023)
- **Currency**: Israeli new shekel (₪, ILS)
- **Official Languages**: Hebrew, Arabic
- **Calling Code**: +972
- **Internet TLD**: .il
- **Region**: Asia (Middle East)

## Key Facts
- Known globally as "Startup Nation" with ~8,000 tech startups and 100+ unicorns.
- High-tech sector accounts for 15% of GDP and 50% of exports.
- Mandatory military service: 32 months for men, 24 months for women.
- Highest water recycling rate globally (90%).
- Home to notable tech companies including Mobileye, Waze, and Check Point.

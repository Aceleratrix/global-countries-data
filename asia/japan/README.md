## 🎯 Key Highlights for AI First Businesses

### 🏙 Tokyo Tech Hub (Japan's Silicon Valley)
- **GDP**: $2.1 trillion (49% of Japan, BIGGER than Silicon Valley's $1.2T!)
- **Population**: 14 million (Tokyo metro)
- **Tech Companies HQ**: Sony ($125B), Rakuten ($25B), Nintendo ($50B), LINE ($18B), Mercari ($6.5B)
- **Venture Capital**: $4.5B deployed (2025), 6 unicorns
- **Average SWE Salary**: ¥6M-18M ($40K-$120K) - 3x LOWER than Silicon Valley!
- **Cloud Regions**: AWS ap-northeast-1 (2ms latency), GCP asia-northeast1 (3ms), Azure Japan East (2ms)

### 💻 AI/Cloud Services (All in Tokyo!)
- **AWS Tokyo** (ap-northeast-1) - 34% market share
- **Google Cloud Tokyo** (asia-northeast1) - 8% market share
- **Azure Japan East** (Tokyo) - 18% market share
- **LINE Clova** (95M users!) - BEST Japanese NLP/voice AI
- **Preferred Networks** (PFN) - Japan's leading AI startup ($1.5B)

### 📜 Legal Framework (Unique to Japan)
- **APPI** (Act on Protection of Personal Information) - Japanese GDPR/LGPD equivalent
  - Max fine: ¥100M ($670K) or 3% revenue
  - 2022 revision: Data portability, breach notification, cross-border rules
- **Society 5.0** - Government initiative for "Super Smart Society" ($2B annual AI investment!)
- **Lifetime Employment** (35% of large firms) - UNIQUE cultural system
- **Non-competes NOT enforceable** - Can join competitors (unlike US Texas!)
- **Golden Week** (April 29 - May 5) - 7-10 days holiday (plan support accordingly!)

### 💳 Payment Methods (UNIQUE to Japan!)
- **Konbini Payments** (25% of e-commerce!) - Pay at 56,000+ convenience stores (7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart)
  - UNIQUE IN THE WORLD! No other developed nation has this!
- **Suica/Pasmo** - Transit cards that DOUBLE as e-money (85% of transit!)
  - UNIQUE: One card for BOTH transit + shopping!
- **LINE Pay** (60M+ users) - QR code payments via LINE app (95% penetration!)
- **Cash Still 15%** of e-commerce - VERY HIGH for developed nation (vs US 5%, Brazil 8%)
- **Credit Cards** - 45% e-commerce (JCB is Japanese brand, 40% domestic)

### 🏢 Company Structures (Japanese Style)
- **Kabushiki Kaisha (KK)** - Standard corporation (like US C-Corp), 85% of formal corps
- **Godo Kaisha (GK)** - LLC equivalent, easier setup (14% of formal corps)
- **Kojin Jigyo** - Sole proprietorship (78% of ALL businesses!)
- **Bonuses** - 4-6 months salary annually (UNIQUE! US companies rarely give bonuses)
- **Stock Options** - 35% of tech companies (still prefer cash bonuses!)

### 💰 Tax Incentives (For AI/Tech)
- **Consumption Tax (VAT)**: 10% (reduced 8% for food)
- **Corporate Tax**: 23.2% effective (national + local)
- **R&D Tax Credit**: 20% for "Innovative R&D" (AI qualifies!) vs standard 6%
- **Special Depreciation**: 50% first-year for AI/GPU equipment
- **Invoice System** (2023) - Must register to issue tax-compliant invoices

### 🌍 Import/Export (Japan's Trade Power)
- **CPTPP Member** - Duty-free from US, Canada, Australia, etc.
- **JEEPA (EU)** - Duty-free from Europe
- **RCEP (China)** - Duty-free from China (but AI chip exports restricted!)
- **Export Controls** - Cannot export NVIDIA H100/A100 to China/Russia (similar to US)
- **De Minimis**: ¥10,000 ($67) - Between Brazil ($50) and US ($800)

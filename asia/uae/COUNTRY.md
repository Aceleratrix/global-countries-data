# 🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates (UAE)
## Basic Information
- ISO 3166-1 Alpha-2: AE
- Capital: Abu Dhabi
- Largest City: Dubai
- Population (2024 est.): 9.9 million
- GDP (2024): $0.50 trillion (26th largest economy globally)
- Currency: UAE Dirham (AED)
- Official Language: Arabic
- Government: Federal absolute monarchy
- Key Industries: Oil & Gas, Tourism, Real Estate, Financial Services, Artificial Intelligence, Logistics

# State of Kuwait (دولة الكويت)

## Basic Information
- **Common Name**: Kuwait
- **Official Name**: State of Kuwait
- **ISO Codes**: KW (alpha-2), KWT (alpha-3), 414 (numeric)
- **Capital**: Kuwait City
- **Population**: ~4.3 million (2024 estimate)
- **Area**: 17,818 km²
- **Currency**: Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD)
- **Languages**: Arabic (official), English widely spoken
- **Calling Code**: +965
- **Time Zone**: UTC+3
- **Continent**: Asia (Western Asia)

## Government
- **Type**: Unitary parliamentary semi-constitutional monarchy
- **Current Emir**: Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah
- **Current Prime Minister**: Ahmad Al-Sabah
- **Independence**: June 19, 1961 (from the United Kingdom)
- **Constitution**: Adopted November 11, 1962

## Economy
- **GDP (Nominal, 2024)**: ~$180 billion USD
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$38,000 USD
- **Main Industries**: Petroleum, natural gas, petrochemicals, fertilizers, construction, financial services
- **Unemployment Rate**: ~2.2% (2024 estimate)

## Demographics
- **Population Density**: ~241 people per km²
- **Urban Population**: 100% (fully urbanized)
- **Religions**: Islam (Sunni ~70%, Shia ~30%), small Christian and Hindu minorities
- **Ethnic Groups**: Kuwaiti Arabs (~30%), other Arabs (~20%), South Asian expatriates (~40%), other expatriates (~10%)

## Geography
- **Terrain**: Flat to slightly undulating desert plains
- **Climate**: Desert; hot, dry summers (up to 50°C) and mild winters
- **Highest Point**: Unnamed elevation in Al Jahra Governorate (306 m)
- **Lowest Point**: Persian Gulf (0 m)
- **Natural Resources**: Petroleum, natural gas, fish, shrimp

## Culture
- **National Anthem**: Al-Nasheed Al-Watani
- **National Days**: February 25 (National Day), February 26 (Liberation Day)
- **Traditional Cuisine**: Machboos, Gabout, Harees
- **Traditional Dress**: Dishdasha, Ghutra, Agal (men); Abaya, Hijab (women)

## Infrastructure
- **Road Network**: ~5,800 km paved
- **Airports**: 4 (including Kuwait International Airport)
- **Internet Penetration**: 99% (2024)
- **Railway Network**: No active railway system

# Kuwait 🇰🇼

## Overview
- **Official Name**: State of Kuwait
- **Capital**: Kuwait City (Population: ~500,000)
- **Population**: ~4.3 million (2024 estimate)
- **Area**: 17,818 km²
- **Currency**: Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD)
- **Languages**: Arabic (official), English (widely spoken)
- **Government**: Constitutional emirate
- **GDP**: $0.15 trillion (74th largest economy globally, 2024 estimate)

## Economy
Kuwait has a high-income economy driven by oil reserves, which account for approximately 90% of export revenues and 50% of GDP. Key sectors include petroleum, petrochemicals, finance, and construction.

## Demographics
The population is ethnically diverse, with around 60% being expatriates. Major ethnic groups include Kuwaiti Arabs, other Arab nationals, South Asians, and Southeast Asians.

## Geography
Located in Western Asia at the northern tip of the Persian Gulf, Kuwait is bordered by Iraq to the north and Saudi Arabia to the south. The terrain is mostly flat desert with few low-lying hills.

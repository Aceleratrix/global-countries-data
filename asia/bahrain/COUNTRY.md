# Bahrain 🇧🇭

## Overview
- **Official Name**: Kingdom of Bahrain
- **Region**: Western Asia (Middle East)
- **GDP**: $0.15 Trillion USD (75th largest economy globally)
- **Population**: ~1.5 million (2024 estimate)
- **Capital**: Manama (population ~200,000 city proper)
- **Official Language**: Arabic
- **Currency**: Bahraini Dinar (BHD)
- **Government**: Constitutional Monarchy
- **Independence**: 15 August 1971 (from the United Kingdom)

## Key Facts
- Located in the Persian Gulf, an archipelago of 33 islands
- Major industries: Oil and gas, aluminum smelting, financial services, tourism
- Home to the Bahrain International Circuit, host of the Formula 1 Gulf Air Bahrain Grand Prix
- UNESCO World Heritage Site: Bahrain Fort (Qal'at al-Bahrain)

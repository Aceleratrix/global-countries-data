## Important Notes for AI Agents

### 🚨 Critical Cultural & Regulatory Considerations
1. **Internet Censorship:** Google, Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, YouTube are BLOCKED
2. **Data Localization:** Must store Chinese user data within China (PIPL)
3. **AI Content Regulation:** All AI-generated content must be labeled and traceable
4. **Encryption Restrictions:** Strict controls on VPNs and encryption tools
5. **Business Culture:** Guanxi (relationships) critical; face-saving (面子) important
6. **Payment Ecosystem:** Cash nearly extinct in cities; QR code payments dominate
7. **Mobile-First:** 99% of digital services accessed via smartphone apps
8. **Super Apps:** WeChat integrates chat, payments, gov services, e-commerce

### Integration Guidance
- **Use Chinese alternatives:** Baidu (search), WeChat (messaging), Douyin (video)
- **Local cloud deployment:** Must use domestic cloud providers for Chinese users
- **Compliance:** PIPL, CSL, and Data Security Law (DSL) all apply
- **Payment integration:** WeChat Pay + Alipay mandatory for e-commerce
- **Content review:** All user-generated content must be pre-moderated
- **VPN restrictions:** Corporate VPNs require government approval

# Kazakhstan 🇰🇿
## Official Name: Republic of Kazakhstan
## Basic Facts
- **Capital**: Astana
- **Population**: 20,426,568 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 2,724,900 km² (9th largest country globally)
- **GDP**: $0.06T (137th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Kazakhstani tenge (₸, KZT)
- **Languages**: Kazakh (official), Russian (official in administrative contexts)
- **Region**: Central Asia (Asia continent)
- **Landlocked**: Yes
- **Borders**: China, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan
- **Timezones**: UTC+05:00, UTC+06:00
- **Gini Index**: 27.8 (2018)
- **Calling Code**: +7 (suffixes 6,7)
- **Top-Level Domains**: .kz, .қаз
- **FIFA Code**: KAZ
- **Car Driving Side**: Right
- **Coordinates**: 48.0196°N, 66.9237°E

## Economy
- GDP: $0.06 trillion (137th globally)
- Primary industries: Oil and gas, mining, agriculture, manufacturing
- Key exports: Crude oil, metals, grain

## Geography
- Largest landlocked country globally
- Terrain: Vast steppes, mountains (Tian Shan in southeast), deserts (Kyzylkum in south)
- Major rivers: Ural, Syr Darya, Ili
- Major lakes: Caspian Sea (shores), Balkhash, Zaysan

## Demographics
- Ethnic groups: Kazakh (68%), Russian (20%), Uzbek, Ukrainian, German, others
- Religion: Islam (70%), Christianity (26%), others
- Urbanization: ~58% urban population

## Government
- Form: Unitary presidential republic
- Current President: Kassym-Jomart Tokayev (as of 2024)
- Legislature: Bicameral Parliament (Senate and Mazhilis)

## International Relations
- Member of: UN, CIS, CSTO, SCO, OIC, EAEU
- Strategic partners: Russia, China, EU, US

# Bhutan 🇧🇹

## Basic Information
- Official Name: Kingdom of Bhutan
- ISO Codes: BT (Alpha-2), BTN (Alpha-3)
- Capital: Thimphu
- Largest City: Thimphu
- Population: 770,000 (2023 estimate)
- Area: 38,394 km²
- Currency: Ngultrum (BTN)
- Official Language: Dzongkha
- Government Type: Constitutional Monarchy
- Current Monarch: Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck (5th Dragon King)
- Current Prime Minister: Lotay Tshering (as of 2024)
- Independence: Never colonized; modern state established in 1907

## Geography
Landlocked country in the Eastern Himalayas, bordered by China (Tibet Autonomous Region) to the north and India to the south, east, and west. Features subtropical plains in the south and high Himalayan peaks in the north, including Gangkhar Puensum (7,570m), the world's highest unclimbed mountain.

## Demographics
- Ethnic Groups: Ngalop (Western Bhutanese, ~50%), Sharchop (Eastern Bhutanese, ~35%), Lhotshampa (Southern Bhutanese, ~15%)
- Religions: Vajrayana Buddhism (state religion, ~75%), Hinduism (~22%), others ~3%
- Life Expectancy: 72 years (2023 estimate)
- Literacy Rate: 66% (2017 census)

## Economy
Based on agriculture, forestry, tourism, and hydropower exports to India. Uses Gross National Happiness (GNH) index instead of GDP as primary development metric.

## Culture
Known for traditional architecture, masked Cham dances, and strict high-value low-impact tourism policy. National symbols: Takin (animal), Raven (bird), Blue Poppy (flower).

# Thailand 🇹🇭
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Kingdom of Thailand
- **Capital**: Bangkok (metropolitan population: ~10.5 million)
- **Population**: 71.8 million (2024 UN estimate)
- **GDP**: $0.49 trillion (27th largest economy globally, 2024 IMF)
- **Currency**: Thai Baht (THB, ฿)
- **Official Language**: Thai
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary system
- **Key Statistics**: Tourism contributes ~20% of GDP; world's largest rice exporter; ASEAN member state.

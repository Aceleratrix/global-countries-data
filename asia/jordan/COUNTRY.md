# Jordan

- **Official Name**: Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan
- **Region**: Asia (Middle East)
- **GDP**: $0.14T (76th largest economy globally)
- **Population**: 11.3 million (2023 estimate)
- **Capital**: Amman (metro population ~4 million)
- **Currency**: Jordanian Dinar (JOD)
- **Official Language**: Arabic
- **Government**: Constitutional monarchy
- **Head of State**: King Abdullah II
- **Head of Government**: Prime Minister Jafar Hassan
- **Area**: 89,342 km²
- **ISO Code**: JO
- **Calling Code**: +962
- **Borders**: Syria, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Israel, Palestine

## Key Facts
- Home to Petra, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Dead Sea, the lowest point on Earth (-431 m)
- Wadi Rum, a protected desert wilderness
- Stable economy with strong tourism and phosphate industries

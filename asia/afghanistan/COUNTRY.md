# Afghanistan
## General Information
- **Official Name**: Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan
- **ISO 3166-1 Alpha-2**: AF
- **ISO 3166-1 Alpha-3**: AFG
- **Capital**: Kabul
- **Largest City**: Kabul
- **Currency**: Afghan afghani (AFN, ؋)
- **Population (2023 Estimate)**: 41,125,737
- **Area**: 652,864 km²
- **Languages**: Pashto (official), Dari (Persian, official)
- **Religion**: Islam (Sunni 85-90%, Shia 10-15%)
- **Government Type**: De facto Islamic Emirate (Taliban-led, since August 2021)
- **Continent**: Asia
- **Region**: South Asia
- **Borders**: Pakistan, Iran, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, China (Wakhan Corridor)

# Turkey (Türkiye)

## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Ankara
- **Largest City**: Istanbul (15,066,000 population, 2024)
- **Population**: 85,372,377 (2024 estimate)
- **GDP (PIB)**: $1.03 trillion (2024, nominal)
- **GDP Rank**: 18th largest economy globally
- **Currency**: Turkish Lira (TRY)
- **Official Language**: Turkish
- **Country Code**: +90
- **ISO 3166-1 Alpha-2**: TR
- **Region**: Transcontinental (Anatolia/Asia Minor and Eastern Thrace in Europe)
- **Government**: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
- **Area**: 783,356 km²
- **Time Zone**: UTC+3 (TRT)

## Economic Overview
Turkey is the 18th largest economy globally with a GDP of $1.03 trillion. The economy is diversified with strong automotive, textiles, electronics, and tourism sectors. Istanbul serves as the country's financial hub.

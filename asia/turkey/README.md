# Turkey Data Repository 🇹🇷

This directory contains 13 mandatory data files for Turkey, the world's 18th largest economy with $1.03 trillion GDP.

## Files Included
1. `COUNTRY.md` - Basic country information
2. `legal/internet-law.yaml` - Internet legislation and regulations
3. `legal/labor-law.yaml` - Labor laws and worker protections
4. `fiscal/tax-codes.yaml` - Tax codes and fiscal regulations
5. `financial/banks.yaml` - Major banking institutions
6. `telecom/phone-formats.yaml` - Telephone number formats
7. `demographics/population.yaml` - Population statistics
8. `tech/ai-cloud-services.yaml` - AI and cloud service providers
9. `business/company-sizes.yaml` - Company size classifications
10. `business/real-estate.yaml` - Real estate market data
11. `business/import-export.yaml` - Import/export regulations and data
12. `marketing/digital-marketing.yaml` - Digital marketing landscape

## Data Sources
- Turkish Statistical Institute (TUIK)
- Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey (TCMB)
- Revenue Administration (GİB)
- Information and Communication Technologies Authority (BTK)

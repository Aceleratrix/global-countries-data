# Qatar 🇶🇦

## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Doha (population ~1 million)
- **Population**: 2.7 million (2023 estimate)
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.27 trillion (51st largest economy globally)
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$94,000 (one of the highest in the world)
- **Currency**: Qatari Riyal (QAR)
- **Official Language**: Arabic (English widely spoken)
- **Region**: Asia (Western Asia)
- **Government**: Absolute monarchy (Emirate)
- **Current Emir**: Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani
- **Independence**: September 3, 1971 (from the United Kingdom)

## Key Achievements
- Host of the 2022 FIFA World Cup
- World's largest exporter of liquefied natural gas (LNG)
- Home to Hamad International Airport (ranked world's best airport multiple times)

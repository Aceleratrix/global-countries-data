# Bangladesh (🇧🇩)
**Official Name**: People's Republic of Bangladesh  
**Capital**: Dhaka  
**Largest City**: Dhaka  
**Population**: ~169.8 million (2023 estimate)  
**Area**: 148,460 km² (57,320 sq mi)  
**Currency**: Bangladeshi Taka (BDT, ৳)  
**Official Language**: Bengali (Bangla)  
**Government**: Unitary parliamentary constitutional republic  
**Independence**: March 26, 1971 (from Pakistan)  
**Calling Code**: +880  
**Internet TLD**: .bd  

**Key Facts**:
- 8th most populous country in the world
- Located in South Asia, bordered by India (north, east, west) and Myanmar (southeast), with a coastline along the Bay of Bengal
- Major rivers: Ganges (Padma), Brahmaputra (Jamuna), Meghna
- Economy driven by agriculture (rice, jute), textiles, and remittances
- 91% Muslim, 8% Hindu, 0.6% Buddhist, 0.4% Christian population

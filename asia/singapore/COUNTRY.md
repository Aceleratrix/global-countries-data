# Singapore
**Capital:** Singapore (city-state)
**Population:** 5,703,600 (2023 estimate)
**GDP:** $0.35 trillion (2023, 40th largest economy globally)
**Official Languages:** English, Malay, Mandarin, Tamil
**Currency:** Singapore Dollar (SGD)
**Region:** Southeast Asia
**Government:** Unitary parliamentary republic
**Area:** 733.1 km²
**Time Zone:** UTC+8 (Singapore Standard Time)
**Calling Code:** +65
**Internet TLD:** .sg

## Key Facts
- Highly developed free-market economy, major financial and shipping hub
- One of the world's busiest ports, home to Singapore Changi Airport (consistently ranked top airport globally)
- Universal healthcare system (MediShield Life)
- Compulsory education up to secondary level, high literacy rate (~97%)
- Multicultural society with significant Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Eurasian populations

# 🇳🇱 Netherlands (The Netherlands)

**Capital:** Amsterdam (Government: The Hague)  
**Population:** 17.9 million (2024)  
**Currency:** Euro (EUR, €)  
**Official Language:** Dutch (Nederlands)  
**Government:** Constitutional Monarchy + Parliamentary Democracy  
**GDP (Nominal):** $1.09 trillion (2024) – 17th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $61,480  
**HDI:** 0.941 (Very High) – 8th globally  
**Time Zone:** UTC+1 (CET)  

## 🌍 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** NL (Alpha-2), NLD (Alpha-3), 528 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .nl
- **Calling Code:** +31
- **EU Member:** Yes (founding member 1952)
- **NATO Member:** Yes
- **Schengen Area:** Yes

## 🏢 Business Environment

The Netherlands is a major European logistics and trade hub, home to the Port of Rotterdam (Europe's largest port). Strong sectors in agriculture (2nd largest agri-exporter), energy, tech, and water management.

**Key Industries:**
- Agri-food & Horticulture (world's 2nd largest agri-exporter)
- Energy (Shell, renewable transition)
- Logistics & Transport (Port of Rotterdam, Schiphol Airport)
- Technology & Semiconductors (ASML, NXP)
- Financial Services (ING, ABN AMRO, Rabobank)
- Water Management & Engineering
- Creative Industries & Design

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Netherlands ranks among top EU countries for AI readiness:
- **AI Delta Plan** – National AI coalition
- **Tu/e (Eindhoven)** – AI Systems Institute
- **QUVA** – QUVA Lab (UvA + Qualcomm)
- **AI Hub Amsterdam** – AI for health, mobility, energy

**Major Tech/AI Companies:**
- **ASML** – World's only supplier of EUV lithography systems ($300B market cap)
- **Booking.com** – AI-driven travel platform (HQ in Amsterdam)
- **Adyen** – AI-powered payment processing ($50B market cap)
- **TomTom** – AI navigation & geospatial data
- **Bird** – AI weather forecasting

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 3.4% (2024, near historic low)
- **Inflation Rate:** 2.8% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 25.8% (19% for first €200K)
- **Minimum Wage:** €13.68/hour (2024, age 21+)
- **GDP Growth:** 1.6% (2024)

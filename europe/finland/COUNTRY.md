# Finland 🇫🇮
## Key Facts
- **GDP**: $0.33 trillion (43rd largest economy globally)
- **Population**: ~5.6 million (2024 estimate)
- **Capital**: Helsinki (metropolitan area: ~1.3 million)
- **Official Languages**: Finnish, Swedish
- **Currency**: Euro (EUR)
- **EU Member**: Yes (since 1995)
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary republic

## Additional Information
Finland is known for its high quality of life, extensive welfare system, and leadership in technology and education. It has the highest number of heavy metal bands per capita globally and a strong sauna culture.

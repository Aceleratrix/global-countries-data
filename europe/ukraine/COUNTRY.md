# Ukraine 🇺🇦

## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Kyiv (population ~3 million)
- **Population**: ~37 million (2024 UN estimate, adjusted for war displacement)
- **GDP**: $0.27 trillion (50th largest economy globally, 2023 IMF data)
- **Region**: Eastern Europe
- **Country Code**: UA
- **Currency**: Ukrainian hryvnia (UAH)
- **Official Language**: Ukrainian
- **Independence**: August 24, 1991 (from the Soviet Union)

## Key Facts
- Major global agricultural exporter (wheat, corn, sunflower oil)
- Rich natural resources: iron ore, coal, natural gas
- Pre-war IT sector contributed ~4% of GDP
- Member of the United Nations, OSCE, GUAM, and Council of Europe
- 3 UNESCO World Heritage Sites: Kyiv Pechersk Lavra, Saint-Sophia Cathedral, Lviv Historic Centre

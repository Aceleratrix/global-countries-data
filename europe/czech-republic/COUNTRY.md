# Czech Republic (Czechia)
**Flag:** 🇨🇿  
**Capital:** Prague  
**Population:** 10.5 million (2024 est.)  
**GDP:** $0.43 trillion (31st largest economy globally, 2024 est.)  
**Currency:** Czech koruna (CZK)  
**Official Language:** Czech  
**EU Member:** Yes (2004)  
**Schengen Area:** Yes  

## Key Facts
- Major Industry: Automotive (Skoda Auto is the largest employer), machinery, beer production (Pilsner Urquell originates here)
- Top Export: Motor vehicles and parts
- Notable Landmarks: Prague Castle, Charles Bridge, Český Krumlov
- Famous For: Pilsner beer, Bohemian crystal, classical music (Antonín Dvořák, Bedřich Smetana)

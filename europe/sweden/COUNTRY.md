# Sweden (Kingdom of Sweden)

## Basic Information
- Official Name: Kingdom of Sweden
- Capital: Stockholm
- Largest City: Stockholm
- Area: 450,295 km²
- Population (2024 estimate): ~10.5 million
- Currency: Swedish krona (SEK)
- Official Language: Swedish
- Government: Unitary parliamentary constitutional monarchy
- Monarch: Carl XVI Gustaf
- Prime Minister (2024): Ulf Kristersson

## Key Facts
- Borders Norway and Finland; maritime border with Denmark
- Member of the European Union (1995), NATO (2024)
- High standard of living, universal tax-funded healthcare and education
- 70% of land area covered by forests
- Known for ABBA, IKEA, Spotify, and leading environmental sustainability efforts
- Met 2020 Kyoto Protocol target (40% greenhouse gas reduction)

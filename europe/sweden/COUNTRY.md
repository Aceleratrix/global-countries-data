# Sweden (Sverige)
- **Capital**: Stockholm
- **Population**: 10.5 million (2023 est.)
- **GDP**: $0.58 trillion (2023, 22nd/24th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Swedish Krona (SEK)
- **Official Language**: Swedish
- **Major Companies**: Spotify, Skype, Ericsson, Volvo, IKEA, H&M
- **Key Facts**: High standard of living, welfare state model, member of EU, EEA, Schengen Area.

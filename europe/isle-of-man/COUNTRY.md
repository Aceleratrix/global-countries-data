# 🇮🇫 Isle of Man (Ilha de Man)

**Capital:** Douglas  
**Population:** ~84,000  
**Area:** 572 km²  
**Currency:** Manx pound (IMP, pegged to GBP)  
**Languages:** English, Manx Gaelic  
**Government:** Parliamentary constitutional monarchy (British Crown dependency)  

## Quick Facts
- Oldest continuous parliament in the world: Tynwald (over 1000 years)
- Famous for the Isle of Man TT motorcycle races
- Self-governing with its own laws, tax system, and courts
- Located in the Irish Sea between Great Britain and Ireland

## Economy
- Offshore banking and financial services
- Tourism (especially TT races)
- Information technology
- Manufacturing (aerospace, electronics)
- Agriculture (sheep farming, fisheries)

# Romania
- **GDP**: $0.40T (32nd largest economy globally)
- **Population**: ~19.1 million (2024 estimate)
- **Capital**: Bucharest (~2 million metro population)
- **ISO Code**: RO
- **Currency**: Romanian Leu (RON)
- **Official Language**: Romanian
- **Key Memberships**: European Union, NATO, UN, Council of Europe
- **Geography**: Located in Southeast Europe, bordered by Bulgaria, Serbia, Hungary, Ukraine, Moldova, and the Black Sea
- **Notable Facts**: Home to the Carpathian Mountains, Danube River, and Transylvania region; largest EU producer of corn and sunflower seeds

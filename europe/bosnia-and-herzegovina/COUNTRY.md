# Bosnia and Herzegovina 🇧🇦🇧🇿
## Overview
Bosnia and Herzegovina (BiH) is a country in Southeast Europe, located on the Balkan Peninsula. It borders Croatia to the north, west, and south; Serbia to the east; and Montenegro to the southeast. It has a narrow coastline along the Adriatic Sea (approximately 20 km). The country is a parliamentary republic composed of two entities: the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina (FBiH) and Republika Srpska (RS), plus the Brčko District, a self-governing administrative unit.

## Key Facts
- Capital: Sarajevo
- Largest city: Sarajevo
- Official languages: Bosnian, Serbian, Croatian
- Demonym: Bosnian, Herzegovinian
- Government: Parliamentary republic
- Area: 51,129 km²
- Population: ~3.5 million (2023 estimate)
- Currency: Bosnia and Herzegovina convertible mark (BAM)
- Time zone: CET (UTC+1), CEST (UTC+2) in summer
- Calling code: +387
- ISO 3166 code: BA (alpha-2), BIH (alpha-3), 070 (numeric)

# Hungary 🇭🇺

## Overview
- **Official Name**: Hungary
- **Capital**: Budapest
- **Population**: 9,600,000 (2024 estimate)
- **Area**: 93,030 km²
- **GDP**: $0.26 trillion (52nd largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Hungarian Forint (HUF)
- **Language**: Hungarian (Magyar)
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary republic
- **EU Member**: Yes (2004)
- **NATO Member**: Yes (1999)
- **Schengen Area**: Yes

## Key Facts
- Landlocked country in Central Europe
- Known for its thermal springs, Danube River, and rich cultural heritage
- Prime Minister: Viktor Orbán (2010–present)
- President: Tamás Sulyok (2024–present)

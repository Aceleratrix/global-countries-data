# Slovakia (Slovak Republic)

## Overview
Slovakia, officially the Slovak Republic (Slovenská republika), is a landlocked country in Central Europe. It is the world's 92nd largest economy with a GDP (PPP) of $0.11 trillion.

## Key Facts
- **Common Name**: Slovakia
- **Official Name**: Slovak Republic (Slovenská republika)
- **ISO Codes**: SK (CCA2), SVK (CCA3)
- **Capital**: Bratislava
- **Region**: Europe
- **Subregion**: Central Europe
- **Population**: 5,413,813 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 49,037 km²
- **GDP (PPP)**: $0.11 trillion (92nd global rank)
- **Currency**: Euro (EUR, €)
- **Official Language**: Slovak
- **Timezone**: UTC+01:00 (CET)
- **Borders**: Austria (AUT), Czech Republic (CZE), Hungary (HUN), Poland (POL), Ukraine (UKR)
- **Landlocked**: Yes
- **EU Member**: Yes (2004)
- **NATO Member**: Yes (2004)
- **Euro Adopted**: 2009

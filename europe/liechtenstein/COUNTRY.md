# Liechtenstein 🇱🇮

## Basic Information
- **Common Name**: Liechtenstein
- **Official Name**: Principality of Liechtenstein
- **Capital**: Vaduz
- **Population**: 39,327 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 160 km²
- **Currency**: Swiss franc (CHF) (official, though Liechtenstein issues its own coins)
- **Languages**: German (official)
- **Region**: Europe
- **Subregion**: Western Europe
- **Borders**: Switzerland, Austria
- **ISO 3166-1 Code**: LI
- **Top-Level Domain**: .li
- **Calling Code**: +423

## Economy
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.05T (2023 estimate)
- **Global GDP Rank**: 178th largest economy
- **Key Industries**: Manufacturing (precision instruments, dental products), financial services, tourism

## Government
- **Form of Government**: Constitutional monarchy (principality)
- **Head of State**: Prince Hans-Adam II (with hereditary succession)
- **Head of Government**: Prime Minister Daniel Risch (as of 2024)

## Geography
- **Terrain**: Mostly mountainous (Alps) with Rhine Valley in the west
- **Climate**: Continental, with cold winters and mild summers
- **Highest Point**: Grauspitz (2,599 m)
- **Lowest Point**: Ruggeller Riet (430 m)

## Demographics
- **Ethnic Groups**: Liechtensteiner (65%), Swiss, Austrian, German, Italian (35%)
- **Religion**: Christianity (majority Roman Catholic, minority Protestant)
- **Life Expectancy**: 82.3 years (2023 estimate)

## Symbols
- **Flag**: Two horizontal blue and red stripes with a gold crown on the blue stripe
- **Coat of Arms**: Crown, eagle, and other heraldic elements representing the prince's authority
- **National Anthem**: "Oben am jungen Rhein" ("High on the Young Rhine")

# Jersey (🇯🇪)

## Basic Information
- **Common Name**: Jersey
- **Official Name**: Bailiwick of Jersey
- **Capital**: Saint Helier
- **Country Codes**: JE (cca2), JEY (cca3), 832 (ccn3)
- **Currency**: British Pound Sterling (GBP, £)
- **Population**: ~103,267 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 118 km²

## Geography
Jersey is the largest of the Channel Islands, located in the English Channel near the coast of Normandy, France. It is a British Crown dependency with self-governing status.

## Economy
The economy is dominated by financial services, tourism, and agriculture. Jersey is a major offshore financial center.

## Languages
Official languages are English and French. Jèrriais, a Norman language, is recognized as a regional language.

## Taxation & Fiscal

- **Corporate Tax:** 25% (from April 2023, was 19%)
- **VAT (Value Added Tax):** 20% (standard), 5% (reduced - energy, home improvements), 0% (food, books, children's clothing)
- **Income Tax:** 0-45% (progressive, personal allowance £12,570)
- **Digital Services Tax (DST):** 2% on UK revenues >£500M
- **Tax Authority:** HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC)

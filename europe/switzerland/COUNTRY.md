# Switzerland (Schweiz/Suisse/Svizzera/Svizra)
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Swiss Confederation
- **Local Names**: Schweiz (German), Suisse (French), Svizzera (Italian), Svizra (Romansh)
- **ISO 3166-1 Alpha-2 Code**: CH
- **ISO 3166-1 Alpha-3 Code**: CHE
- **Capital**: Bern
- **Largest City**: Zürich
- **Population (2024 Estimate)**: 8,795,166
- **Area**: 41,285 km²
- **Currency**: Swiss franc (CHF)
- **Official Languages**: German, French, Italian, Romansh
- **Government Type**: Federal republic, direct democracy
- **Head of State**: Guy Parmelin (President of the Swiss Confederation, 2026)
- **Time Zone**: Central European Time (UTC+1) / Central European Summer Time (UTC+2)
- **Calling Code**: +41
- **Internet TLD**: .ch, .swiss

## Key Facts
- Landlocked country in Central Europe bordered by Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein
- Home to many international organizations including the United Nations Office at Geneva, WHO, ILO, and WTO
- Neutral since 1815, not a member of the European Union
- High Human Development Index (ranked 2nd globally in 2023-2024)
- Famous for banking, watchmaking, chocolate, cheese, and precision manufacturing

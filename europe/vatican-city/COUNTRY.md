# Vatican City (Holy See)

## Overview
- **Official Name**: State of Vatican City (Stato della Città del Vaticano)
- **Common Name**: Vatican City
- **Capital**: Vatican City
- **Population**: ~825 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 0.44 km² (0.17 sq mi)
- **GDP**: $0.05 trillion (180th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Euro (€)
- **Official Languages**: Italian, Latin
- **Calling Code**: +379
- **Internet TLD**: .va
- **Government Type**: Absolute elective monarchy (Pope as head of state)
- **Bordering Countries**: Italy (surrounded entirely)

## Key Facts
- Smallest sovereign state in the world by both area and population.
- Headquarters of the Catholic Church.
- Entire territory is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- Independent since 1929 via the Lateran Treaty with Italy.
- No airports, railways, or major industrial sectors.
- Economy relies primarily on tourism, donations, and cultural institution revenues.

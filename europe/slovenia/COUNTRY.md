# Slovenia (🇸🇮)

**Official Name**: Republic of Slovenia  
**Capital**: Ljubljana  
**Population**: 2,108,708 (2023 estimate)  
**Area**: 20,271 km²  
**Currency**: Euro (EUR)  
**Languages**: Slovene (official)  
**Calling Code**: +386  
**ISO Codes**: SI (alpha-2), SVN (alpha-3)  
**EU Member**: Yes (since 2004)  
**Schengen Member**: Yes (since 2007)  
**NATO Member**: Yes (since 2004)  

## Quick Facts
- Landlocked country in Central Europe, bordered by Italy, Austria, Hungary, and Croatia.
- Home to Lake Bled, a major tourist destination.
- One of the most forested countries in Europe, with 58% forest cover.

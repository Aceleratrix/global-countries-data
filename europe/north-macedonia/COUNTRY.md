# Republic of North Macedonia 🇲🇰
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of North Macedonia
- **Local Name**: Република Северна Македонија (Macedonian), Republika e Maqedonisë së Veriut (Albanian)
- **Capital**: Skopje
- **Largest City**: Skopje
- **Population**: 1,836,713 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 25,713 km²
- **Currency**: Macedonian denar (MKD, den)
- **Languages**: Macedonian (official), Albanian (co-official in municipalities with >20% Albanian population)
- **Demonym**: Macedonian / North Macedonian
- **ISO Codes**: MK (alpha-2), MKD (alpha-3), 807 (numeric)
- **Internet TLD**: .mk
- **Calling Code**: +389

## Key Facts
- Independence: 8 September 1991 (from Yugoslavia)
- NATO Membership: 27 March 2020
- EU Candidate Status: 17 December 2005
- Prespa Agreement: 12 June 2018 (resolved name dispute with Greece, renamed from "Republic of Macedonia")
- Borders: Greece, Bulgaria, Serbia, Kosovo, Albania
- Ethnic Groups (2021 census): Macedonian 58.4%, Albanian 24.3%, Turkish 3.9%, Roma 2.5%, Serbian 1.3%, other 9.6%
- Religion: Eastern Orthodox Christianity (60.4%), Islam (32.2%), other/none 7.4% (2021)

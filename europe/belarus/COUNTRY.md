# Belarus 🇧🇾
## Country Overview
- **Full Name**: Republic of Belarus
- **Capital**: Minsk (Population: ~2 million)
- **Population**: 9.1 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 207,600 km²
- **GDP**: $0.20 trillion (61st largest economy globally)
- **GDP per capita**: ~$22,000
- **Currency**: Belarusian Ruble (BYN)
- **Official Languages**: Belarusian, Russian
- **Independence**: August 25, 1991 (from the Soviet Union)
- **Current President**: Alexander Lukashenko
- **Borders**: Russia, Ukraine, Poland, Lithuania, Latvia
- **Region**: Europe

## Key Economic Indicators
- Services: ~50% of GDP
- Industry: ~40% of GDP (machinery, chemicals, textiles)
- Agriculture: ~10% of GDP
- Major exports: Machinery, chemicals, agricultural products, refined oil
- Inflation rate (2023): 5.2%
- Unemployment rate (2023): 4.1%

## Demographics
- Population growth rate: -0.3% (declining)
- Urban population: 78%
- Age structure: 0-14 (16%), 15-64 (69%), 65+ (15%)
- Life expectancy: 74 years (male 69, female 79)
- Literacy rate: 99.7%

## Geography
- Highest point: Dzyarzhynskaya Hara (345m)
- Major rivers: Dnieper, Neman, Pripyat
- Major natural feature: Białowieża Forest (UNESCO World Heritage Site, shared with Poland)

## Key Facts
- Universal public healthcare system
- 9-year compulsory education, 50+ higher education institutions
- Active military personnel: 45,000
- Annual tourist arrivals: 1.2 million
- Visa-free entry for most EU and CIS countries

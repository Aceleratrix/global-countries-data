# Bulgaria 🇧🇬
## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Sofia (Population: ~1.3 million)
- **Population**: ~6.9 million (2024 estimate)
- **Area**: 110,994 km²
- **GDP**: $0.21T (60th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Bulgarian Lev (BGN)
- **Official Language**: Bulgarian (Cyrillic script)
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary republic
- **EU Member**: Yes (since 2007)
- **NATO Member**: Yes (since 2004)

## Quick Facts
- Borders: Romania (north), Serbia (west), North Macedonia (southwest), Greece (south), Turkey (southeast)
- Highest Peak: Musala (2,925 m)
- Longest River: Danube (forms northern border)
- Major Industries: Mining, metallurgy, agriculture, tourism, IT, textiles
- UNESCO World Heritage Sites: 8 total (Rila Monastery, Boyana Church, Madara Rider, etc.)
- Life Expectancy: 74.5 years (2024)
- Literacy Rate: 98.4%

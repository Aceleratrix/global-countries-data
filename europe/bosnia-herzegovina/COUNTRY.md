# Bosnia and Herzegovina

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Bosnia and Herzegovina
- **Local Name**: Bosna i Hercegovina / Босна и Херцеговина
- **Capital**: Sarajevo
- **Population**: ~3.5 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 51,197 km²
- **Currency**: Bosnia and Herzegovina convertible mark (BAM)
- **ISO Code**: BIH (Alpha-2: BA, Alpha-3: BIH)
- **Calling Code**: +387
- **Internet TLD**: .ba

## Overview
Bosnia and Herzegovina is a country in Southeastern Europe, located on the Balkan Peninsula. It borders Croatia to the north, west, and south; Serbia to the east; and Montenegro to the southeast. The country has a coastline of about 20 km along the Adriatic Sea.

## Administrative Divisions
Bosnia and Herzegovina consists of two entities:
1. Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina
2. Republika Srpska
Plus the Brčko District, a self-governing administrative unit.

## Key Facts
- Form of Government: Parliamentary republic
- Official Languages: Bosnian, Serbian, Croatian
- Major Religion: Islam (Sunni), Christianity (Orthodox, Catholic)
- GDP (Nominal): ~$23 billion (2023 estimate)
- Major Industries: Steel, coal, automotive parts, textiles, furniture

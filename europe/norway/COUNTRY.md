# Norway (Norge)

## Overview
- Capital: Oslo
- Population: ~5.4 million (2024)
- GDP: $550 billion (24th largest economy globally, 2024)
- Currency: Norwegian Krone (NOK)
- Sovereign Wealth Fund: Government Pension Fund Global ($1.6 trillion, largest sovereign wealth fund globally)
- Key Sectors: Oil & Gas, Seafood, Renewable Energy, Maritime, Technology

## Economy
Norway has a mixed economy with significant state ownership in key sectors like oil, gas, and banking. The country is a major exporter of petroleum and natural gas, which accounts for ~20% of GDP. Post-oil transition policies are driving growth in renewable energy (hydropower, offshore wind) and tech.

## Key Facts
- EU/EEA Member (not EU member, part of European Economic Area)
- High standard of living, top rankings in Human Development Index
- Strong social welfare system

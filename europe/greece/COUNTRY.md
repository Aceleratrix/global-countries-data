- **Capital**: Athens (Metro population: ~4 million)
- **Population**: ~10.4 million (2023 estimate)
- **GDP**: $0.34 trillion (nominal, 2023, 42nd largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Euro (EUR)
- **Official Language**: Greek
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary republic
- **EU Member**: Yes (since 1981)
- **Eurozone Member**: Yes (since 2001)
- **Area**: ~131,957 km²
- **Calling Code**: +30
- **Top-Level Domain**: .gr

- Life Expectancy: ~81 years

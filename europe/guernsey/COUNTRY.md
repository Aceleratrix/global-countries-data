# Guernsey

- **Official Name**: Bailiwick of Guernsey
- **Type**: British Crown Dependency
- **Region**: Europe
- **Subregion**: Western Europe
- **ISO 3166-1 Code**: GG
- **Capital**: St. Peter Port
- **Area**: 78 km² (main island)
- **Population**: ~63,000 (2023 estimate)
- **Currency**: Guernsey pound (GGP), GBP also accepted
- **Languages**: English (official), French, Guernésiais (local Norman dialect)
- **Time Zone**: UTC+0 (GMT), UTC+1 (BST) summer

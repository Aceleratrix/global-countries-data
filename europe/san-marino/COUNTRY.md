# San Marino (🇸🇲)

## Overview
San Marino, officially the Republic of San Marino, is a landlocked microstate enclaved within Italy, located on the Italian Peninsula in Southern Europe. It is the world's fifth-smallest country by area (61 km²) and has a population of ~34,000 as of 2023 estimates. It ranks 176th globally by GDP with a total GDP of ~$0.05 trillion.

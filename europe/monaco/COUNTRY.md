MONACO

BASIC INFORMATION
- Official Name: Principality of Monaco
- ISO 3166-1 Codes: MC (Alpha-2), MCO (Alpha-3), 492 (Numeric)
- Capital: Monaco
- Largest City: Monaco
- Area: 2.02 km² (0.78 sq mi)
- Population (2023): ~36,686
- GDP (Nominal, 2024): $0.05T (179th largest economy globally)
- Currency: Euro (EUR, €)
- Official Language: French
- Government Type: Unitary parliamentary semi-constitutional monarchy
- Head of State: Prince Albert II
- Head of Government: Minister of State (Pierre Dartout)
- Region: Europe
- Subregion: Western Europe

KEY FACTS
- Smallest sovereign state in the world by area (excluding Vatican City)
- Known for casino industry, Formula 1 Grand Prix, tax haven status
- Highest population density of any sovereign state (18,157 people/km²)
- No personal income tax for residents (except French nationals)
- Life expectancy of 89.4 years (highest globally)

# Portugal
## Country Overview
- **Official Name**: Portuguese Republic
- **Capital**: Lisbon (Metro area population: ~3 million)
- **Population**: 10.4 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 92,212 km²
- **GDP**: $0.38 trillion (2023, 34th largest economy globally)
- **GDP per capita**: ~$37,600 (2023)
- **Currency**: Euro (EUR)
- **Language**: Portuguese (Mirandese is co-official in Miranda do Douro)
- **Continent**: Europe
- **Region**: Southern Europe
- **EU Member**: Yes (since 1986)
- **Schengen Area**: Yes
- **Eurozone Member**: Yes (since 1999)
- **Calling Code**: +351
- **Internet TLD**: .pt

## Key Facts
- Parliamentary republic established in 1139, one of Europe's oldest nation-states
- Major global maritime power during the Age of Discovery (15th-16th centuries)
- Colonized territories including Brazil, Angola, Mozambique, and Goa
- Top tourist destination: 30+ million visitors annually (Algarve, Lisbon, Porto, Madeira, Azores)
- World's largest cork producer (50% of global supply)
- 68% urban population, median age 46.2 years
- GDP growth rate: 2.3% (2023)

# Moldova (Republic of Moldova)
## Overview
Moldova, officially the Republic of Moldova, is a landlocked country in Eastern Europe. It borders Romania to the west and Ukraine to the north, east, and south.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Chișinău
- **Population (2024)**: ~2,604,000
- **Area**: 33,846 km²
- **Currency**: Moldovan Leu (MDL)
- **Official Language**: Romanian
- **Calling Code**: +373
- **ISO Code**: MD (Alpha-2), MDA (Alpha-3), 498 (Numeric)
- **Independence**: 27 August 1991 (from the Soviet Union)
- **Government**: Parliamentary republic
- **GDP (2023)**: ~$15.2 billion
- **Borders**: Romania, Ukraine
- **EU Candidate**: Yes (since June 2022)
- **Head of State (2024)**: President Maia Sandu
- **Head of Government (2024)**: Prime Minister Dorin Recean

# Belgium (België/Belgique/Belgien)
**Global Economic Rank:** 23rd (GDP: $0.58 Trillion)  
**Region:** Western Europe  
**Capital:** Brussels  
**Population:** 11.6 Million (2023)  
**Currency:** Euro (EUR)  
**Languages:** Dutch (Flemish), French (Walloon), German  

## Key Facts
- Hosts headquarters of European Union and NATO in Brussels
- Antwerp is Europe's 2nd largest port by container traffic
- Federal constitutional monarchy with parliamentary system
- Founding member of EU, NATO, UN, and Eurozone
- Known for chocolates, waffles, comics (Tintin, Smurfs), and music festivals (Tomorrowland)
- 13 UNESCO World Heritage Sites

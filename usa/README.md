## 🎯 Key Highlights for AI First Businesses

### 🏆 Silicon Valley Ecosystem (San Francisco Bay Area)
- **GDP**: $1.2 trillion (7% of US GDP)
- **Population**: 7.7 million
- **Tech Companies HQ**: Apple ($2.8T), Google ($1.8T), Meta ($800B), Netflix ($250B), NVIDIA ($2.2T), OpenAI ($80B), Anthropic ($18B)
- **Venture Capital**: $89B deployed (2025), 150+ unicorns
- **Average SWE Salary**: $185K base + stock (total comp: $180K-$2M+)
- **Cloud Regions**: AWS us-west-1 (5ms latency to SF), Google us-west2 (8ms), Azure West US (7ms)

### 💻 AI/Cloud Services (All HQ'd in US)
- **OpenAI API** (San Francisco) - GPT-4o, DALL-E 3, Whisper
- **Anthropic Claude** (San Francisco) - Claude 3.5 Sonnet
- **AWS Bedrock** (Seattle/Arlington) - Claude, Titan, Llama 3
- **Google Vertex AI** (Mountain View) - Gemini 1.5 Pro/Ultra
- **Azure AI** (Redmond) - GPT-4, DALL-E 3
- **NVIDIA GPUs** (Santa Clara) - H100, A100 for training

### 📜 Legal Framework (Unique to US)
- **Section 230**: Protects AI platforms from liability for user content
- **No Federal Privacy Law** (unlike LGPD/GDPR) - sectoral approach (HIPAA, GLBA, COPPA)
- **CCPA/CPRA** (California): Privacy rights, $7,500 per violation
- **At-Will Employment**: Fire/hire anytime (except Montana)
- **QSBS (IRC 1202)**: 100% capital gains exclusion (up to $10M) - key for startup founders

### 💳 Payment Methods (Tech Companies Lead)
- **Stripe** (San Francisco) - 45% market share for tech startups
- **PayPal** (San Jose) - 35% market share
- **ACH** - 65% for B2B (like PIX but slower: 1-2 days)
- **No Federal Open Banking** (unlike Brazil's PIX mandate) - market-driven (Plaid, Yodlee)
- **Credit Cards**: 40% of e-commerce (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)

### 🏢 Company Structures (Delaware Dominance)
- **66% of Fortune 500** incorporated in Delaware (business-friendly courts)
- **C-Corp**: Standard for VC-backed startups (double taxation but easy to raise capital)
- **LLC**: Popular for early-stage (pass-through taxation)
- **Stock Options**: 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff, ISO/NSO/RSUs
- **83(b) Election**: Must file within 30 days to avoid AMT

### 💰 Tax Incentives (For AI/Tech)
- **Federal R&D Tax Credit**: 13-20% of qualified expenses (software development, AI training)
- **QSBS**: 100% capital gains exclusion (hold 5+ years, C-Corp <$50M assets)
- **California Competes**: Sales tax exclusions on equipment
- **Texas Enterprise Zone**: Franchise tax credits (Austin - Tesla, Oracle)

### 🌍 Import/Export (AI Chip Controls)
- **BIS Export Controls**: Cannot export NVIDIA H100/A100 to China/Russia
- **Section 301 Tariffs**: 25% on many Chinese tech imports
- **$800 De Minimis**: Imports ≤$800 are duty-free (vs Brazil's $50)
- **HTS Codes**: 8471 (computers), 8542 (semiconductors/GPUs)

# Solomon Islands

## Overview
Solomon Islands is a sovereign country consisting of six major islands and over 900 smaller islands in Oceania, east of Papua New Guinea. It gained independence from the United Kingdom on 7 July 1978.

## Basic Facts
- **Official Name**: Solomon Islands
- **Capital**: Honiara
- **Population**: ~721,000 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 28,896 km²
- **Currency**: Solomon Islands dollar (SBD)
- **Languages**: English (official), Solomon Islands Pijin, 120+ indigenous Austronesian languages
- **Government**: Parliamentary constitutional monarchy; King Charles III is head of state, represented by a Governor-General
- **Independence**: 7 July 1978 (from the United Kingdom)
- **ISO Codes**: Alpha-2: SB, Alpha-3: SLB, Numeric: 090
- **Calling Code**: +677
- **Internet TLD**: .sb

# 🇫🇲 Micronesia (Federated States of Micronesia)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Federated States of Micronesia
- **Capital**: Palikir (Pohnpei State)
- **Population**: ~115,000 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 702 km²
- **Currency**: United States Dollar (USD)
- **Languages**: English (official), Chuukese, Kosraean, Pohnpeian, Yapese
- **Government**: Federal presidential representative democratic republic
- **Independence**: November 3, 1986 (from the United States, under Compact of Free Association)
- **Calling Code**: +691
- **Internet TLD**: .fm

## Geography
Composed of four states: Yap, Chuuk, Pohnpei, and Kosrae, spanning the western Pacific Ocean.

## Key Facts
- Member of the United Nations, Pacific Islands Forum, and other regional organizations
- Largely dependent on US aid under the Compact of Free Association
- Famous for diving sites, including Chuuk Lagoon (wreck diving)
- Susceptible to climate change impacts like sea level rise

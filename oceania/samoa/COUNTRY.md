# Samoa (Independent State of Samoa)
## Overview
Samoa is a Polynesian island country located in the South Pacific Ocean, comprising two main islands (Upolu and Savai'i) and several smaller islets. It gained independence from New Zealand on 1 January 1962 and is a member of the Commonwealth of Nations. The capital and largest city is Apia.

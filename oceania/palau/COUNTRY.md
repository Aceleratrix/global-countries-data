# Palau (🇵🇼)

## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Republic of Palau
- **Country Code**: PW (Alpha-2), PLW (Alpha-3)
- **Capital**: Ngerulmud
- **Region**: Oceania
- **Subregion**: Micronesia
- **Population**: ~18,092 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 459 km²
- **Currency**: United States Dollar (USD, $)
- **Languages**: English (official), Palauan (official)
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.05T (173rd largest economy globally)
- **Independence**: October 1, 1994 (from UN Trusteeship under US administration)

## Summary
Palau is an island country in the western Pacific Ocean, part of the Micronesian region of Oceania. It consists of ~340 islands, with the most populous islands being Angaur, Babeldaob, Koror, and Peleliu. The country is known for its pristine marine biodiversity, particularly the Rock Islands Southern Lagoon, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Palau operates under a presidential representative democratic republic system, with a compact of free association with the United States.

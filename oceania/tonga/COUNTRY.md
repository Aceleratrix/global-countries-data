# Kingdom of Tonga (🇹🇴)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Kingdom of Tonga
- **Capital**: Nuku'alofa
- **Population**: ~107,122 (2023 UN estimate)
- **Area**: 747 km²
- **Official Languages**: Tongan, English
- **Currency**: Tongan pa'anga (TOP)
- **Calling Code**: +676
- **ISO Codes**: TO (alpha-2), TON (alpha-3), 776 (numeric)
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary constitutional monarchy
- **Current Monarch**: Tupou VI
- **Current Prime Minister**: Siaosi Sovaleni

## Overview
Tonga is a Polynesian country consisting of 171 islands, of which 45 are inhabited. It is the only sovereign monarchy in Oceania and was never fully colonized, maintaining its independence throughout the colonial period. It lies south of Samoa and east of Fiji.

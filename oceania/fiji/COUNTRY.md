## 🏢 Business Environment

Fiji has a small island economy focused on tourism, agriculture, and increasingly financial services. The country is positioning itself as a regional hub for business process outsourcing (BPO) and digital services.

**Key Industries:**
- Tourism (major contributor, 40% of GDP pre-pandemic)
- Agriculture (sugar cane, coconuts, ginger, taro)
- Fishing & Aquaculture (tuna processing)
- Manufacturing (garments, food processing)
- Financial Services (regional banking hub)
- Business Process Outsourcing (growing sector)

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Fiji is in the early stages of digital transformation with limited but growing AI adoption:
- **Digital Fiji 2021-2024** – National digital transformation strategy
- Focus on e-government services and digital infrastructure
- Growing BPO sector with potential for AI-augmented services
- University of the South Pacific leading regional tech research
- Challenges: Limited local AI talent, infrastructure constraints

**Major Initiatives:**
- **Fiji National University** – IT and computing programs
- **University of the South Pacific** – Regional tech hub (Laucala Campus)
- **Reserve Bank of Fiji** – Exploring fintech regulations
- **Investment Fiji** – Promoting digital economy investments

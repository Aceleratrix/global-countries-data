## 📈 Key Business Insights

- **Tourism Dependent:** 40% of GDP, recovering post-pandemic (630K+ arrivals 2023)
- **Sugar Industry:** Historical backbone, declining but still significant
- **BPO Growth:** Emerging destination for call centers and back-office operations
- **Regional Hub:** Serving Pacific Island nations from Suva
- **Low Wages:** F$4.68/hour minimum wage (~US$2.10)

## Overview
Vanuatu is an island nation located in the South Pacific Ocean, east of Australia and northeast of New Caledonia. It is an archipelago of approximately 80 islands, with a tropical climate and diverse Melanesian culture. Vanuatu gained independence from joint British-French colonial rule (New Hebrides Condominium) on July 30, 1980.

## Key Facts
- Independence Date: July 30, 1980
- Government Type: Parliamentary republic
- Head of State: President (elected by Parliament for 5-year terms)
- Head of Government: Prime Minister
- Highest Point: Mount Tabwemasana (1,877 m) on Espiritu Santo island
- Main Industries: Tourism, agriculture (copra, cocoa, coffee), fishing, offshore financial services
- Major Religions: Christianity (90%+; Protestant ~70%, Catholic ~20%)
- Ethnic Groups: Ni-Vanuatu 99%, others 1%

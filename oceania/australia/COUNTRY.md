# 🇦🇺 Australia

**Capital:** Canberra  
**Largest City:** Sydney  
**Population:** 27.2 million (2024)  
**Currency:** Australian Dollar (AUD, A$)  
**Official Language:** English  
**Government:** Federal Parliamentary Democracy under Constitutional Monarchy  
**GDP (Nominal):** $1.6 trillion (2024) – 13th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $62,830  
**HDI:** 0.946 (Very High) – 10th globally  
**Time Zone:** AEST (UTC+10), multiple zones  

## 🌏 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** AU (Alpha-2), AUS (Alpha-3), 036 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .au
- **Calling Code:** +61
- **APEC Member:** Yes
- **G20 Member:** Yes
- **Commonwealth Realm:** Yes

## 🏢 Business Environment

Australia has a stable, resource-rich economy with strong sectors in mining, agriculture, education, tourism, and financial services. The country is a regional tech hub with growing AI adoption.

**Key Industries:**
- Mining & Resources (iron ore, coal, gold, lithium)
- Agriculture & Agribusiness (beef, wheat, wine)
- Education Services (international students 700K+)
- Financial Services (Big 4 banks)
- Tourism (Great Barrier Reef, iconic destinations)
- Technology (Silicon Harbour Sydney, Melbourne)

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Australia has a growing AI ecosystem with the **National AI Centre (NAIC)** and **AI Action Plan**:
- $124 million for AI ecosystem (2021-2024)
- Strong focus on AI ethics and responsible AI
- Home to AI research at CSIRO, universities
- Growing startup ecosystem in Sydney and Melbourne

**Major AI Companies/Initiatives:**
- **Commonwealth Bank (CBA)** – AI-driven banking
- **Canva** – AI design tools (valuation $40B)
- **Atlassian** – AI for software teams
- **Airwallex** – AI-powered fintech ($5.5B valuation)
- **CSIRO** – Australia's national science agency (AI research)

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 3.7% (2024, near historical low)
- **Inflation Rate:** 3.2% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 30% (25% for SMEs under $50M revenue)
- **Minimum Wage:** A$23.23/hour (2024, ~US$15.30)
- **GDP Growth:** 1.8% (2024)

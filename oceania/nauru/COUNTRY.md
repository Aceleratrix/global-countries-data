## History

- **Pre-colonial:** Inhabited by Micronesian and Polynesian peoples for over 3,000 years
- **1798:** British navigator John Fearn visits, names it "Pleasant Island"
- **1888:** Annexed by Germany as part of Marshall Islands protectorate
- **1914:** Occupied by Australian forces during WWI
- **1920:** Became League of Nations mandate under Australia, UK, NZ administration
- **1942-1945:** Occupied by Japan during WWII
- **1947:** Became UN Trust Territory under Australia, UK, NZ
- **1968:** Independence as Republic of Nauru
- **1960s-1990s:** Phosphate mining boom, one of world's wealthiest countries per capita
- **2000s:** Phosphate depletion, economic decline, offshore banking controversies

# Ecuador 🇪🇨

## Overview
Ecuador, officially the Republic of Ecuador, is a country in South America bordered by Colombia, Peru, and the Pacific Ocean. It is the 140th largest economy globally with a nominal GDP of $0.06 trillion.

## Basic Facts
- **Official Name**: Republic of Ecuador
- **Common Name**: Ecuador
- **Capital**: Quito
- **Largest City**: Guayaquil
- **Population**: 18,190,487 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 276,841 km²
- **Currency**: United States dollar (USD, $)
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Government Type**: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.06 trillion
- **Global GDP Rank**: 140th

## Geography
- **Region**: South America
- **Subregion**: South America
- **Borders**: Colombia (north), Peru (east and south)
- **Coastline**: 2,237 km along the Pacific Ocean
- **Terrain**: Andes mountains, Amazon rainforest, coastal plains
- **Highest Point**: Chimborazo (6,310 m above sea level)
- **Lowest Point**: Pacific Ocean (0 m)

## Economy
- **Major Industries**: Petroleum extraction, agriculture (bananas, cocoa, coffee, shrimp), tourism, mining
- **Key Exports**: Crude oil, bananas, shrimp, cocoa, coffee
- **Key Imports**: Machinery, vehicles, chemicals, consumer goods

## Demographics
- **Ethnic Composition**: Mestizo (71.9%), Montubio (7.4%), Afro-Ecuadorian (7.2%), Indigenous (7%), White (6.1%), other (0.4%) (2010 census)
- **Religion**: Christianity (74% Roman Catholic, 7.9% Protestant), 18.1% other or non-religious
- **Population Density**: 66 people per km²

# Guyana 🇬🇾
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Co-operative Republic of Guyana
- **Common Name**: Guyana
- **ISO Code**: GY
- **Capital**: Georgetown
- **Population**: 790,681 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 214,969 km²
- **Region**: South America
- **Subregion**: South America
- **Languages**: English (official), Guyanese Creole, Amerindian languages
- **Currency**: Guyanese Dollar (GYD, $)
- **GDP**: $0.05 Trillion (152nd largest economy globally)
- **Borders**: Brazil (BRA), Suriname (SUR), Venezuela (VEN)
- **Timezone**: UTC-04:00
- **Coordinates**: 4.0° N, 58.0° W

## Key Facts
- Only English-speaking country in South America
- Major exports: Crude oil, gold, bauxite, sugar, rice
- Diverse ethnic makeup: East Indian (43%), African (30%), Mixed (17%), Amerindian (9%)
- Recent offshore oil discoveries driving rapid economic growth

# 🇬🇾 Guyana
**Official Name:** Co-operative Republic of Guyana  
**Capital:** Georgetown  
**Population:** 790,326 (2023 UN estimate)  
**Area:** 214,970 km²  
**Currency:** Guyanese dollar (GYD)  
**Official Language:** English  
**Independence:** May 26, 1966 (from the United Kingdom)  
**Current President:** Irfaan Ali (since August 2, 2020)  
**Current Prime Minister:** Mark Phillips (since August 2, 2020)  

## Quick Facts
- South America's only English-speaking sovereign state
- Borders Venezuela (west), Brazil (south), Suriname (east), Atlantic Ocean (north)
- Major rivers: Essequibo, Demerara, Berbice
- Key industries: Offshore oil, gold mining, bauxite, sugar, rice
- GDP (2023 nominal): ~$14.5 billion
- Human Development Index (2021): 0.742 (high)

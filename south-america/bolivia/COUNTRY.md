# Bolivia (Plurinational State of Bolivia) 🇧🇴

## Key Facts
- **Official Name**: Plurinational State of Bolivia
- **Capital**: Sucre (constitutional), La Paz (administrative seat of government)
- **Population**: 12,465,842 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 1,098,581 km² (54th largest country globally)
- **Currency**: Boliviano (BOB, symbol: Bs.)
- **Languages**: Spanish (official), Quechua, Aymara, Guaraní (all official per 2009 constitution)
- **Independence**: August 6, 1825 (from Spain)
- **Calling Code**: +591
- **Internet TLD**: .bo

## Overview
Bolivia is a landlocked central South American country with extreme geographic diversity: the Andes Mountains, Altiplano plateau, and Amazon Basin lowlands. It has one of the highest indigenous population shares in the Americas (~20% identify as Indigenous, per INE 2023). The country is a major natural gas exporter and holds significant mineral reserves (zinc, tin, gold).

# Paraguay (Republic of Paraguay)
**Capital**: Asunción  
**Population**: ~7.45 million (2023 estimate)  
**Area**: 406,752 km²  
**Official Languages**: Spanish, Guaraní  
**Currency**: Paraguayan guaraní (PYG)  
**Government**: Presidential republic  
**President**: Santiago Peña (2023–2028)  
**Independence**: May 14, 1811 (from Spain)  
**Borders**: Argentina (south, southwest), Bolivia (northwest), Brazil (east)  
**Landlocked**: Yes (only landlocked country in South America)  

## Key Facts
- One of two landlocked countries in South America (alongside Bolivia)
- Guaraní is widely spoken by over 90% of the population, making it one of the few indigenous languages with official status in the Americas
- Hosts the Itaipu Dam (shared with Brazil), one of the world's largest hydroelectric power plants
- Major exports: soybeans, beef, hydroelectric power, corn, wheat

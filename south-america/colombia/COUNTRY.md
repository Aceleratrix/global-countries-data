# Colombia 🇨🇴

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Colombia
- **GDP**: $0.33 trillion (44th largest economy globally)
- **Population**: ~52 million (2023 estimate)
- **Capital**: Bogotá (metropolitan area population: ~8 million)
- **Currency**: Colombian Peso (COP)
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Area**: 1,141,748 km²
- **Current President**: Gustavo Petro (elected 2022)
- **Major Cities**: Medellín, Cali, Barranquilla, Cartagena

## Geography
- Borders: Venezuela, Brazil, Peru, Ecuador, Panama
- Coastlines: Caribbean Sea (north), Pacific Ocean (west)
- Mountain ranges: Andes (three branches: Cordillera Occidental, Cordillera Central, Cordillera Oriental)

## Economy
- Main exports: Petroleum, coal, coffee, bananas, cut flowers
- Key sectors: Services, industry, agriculture
- GDP growth (2023): ~1.3%

## Demographics
- Ethnic groups: Mestizo (58%), White (20%), Afro-Colombian (10.6%), Indigenous (3.4%), others
- Religion: Christianity (predominantly Roman Catholic, ~70% of population)

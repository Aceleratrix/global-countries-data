# Peru (Republic of Peru)
## Basic Info
- **ISO Codes**: PE (CCA2), PER (CCA3), 604 (CCN3)
- **Capital**: Lima
- **Region**: South America
- **Subregion**: South America
- **Population**: 33,396,583 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 1,285,216 km²
- **GDP**: $0.06T (138th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Peruvian Sol (PEN)
- **Languages**: Spanish (official), Quechua (official), Aymara (official)
- **Timezone**: UTC-5
- **Borders**: Ecuador, Colombia, Brazil, Bolivia, Chile
- **Flag Emoji**: 🇵🇪

## Key Facts
- Home to the Inca Empire's historic capital, Cusco, and Machu Picchu (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
- Major exports: copper, gold, zinc, textiles, coffee
- Governing system: Presidential representative democratic republic

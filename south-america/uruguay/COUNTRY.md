# Uruguay (Uruguay)
## Oriental Republic of Uruguay
- **Capital**: Montevideo
- **Population**: 3.4 million (2023 UN estimate)
- **GDP**: $0.23 trillion (2023 nominal, 57th largest economy globally)
- **Area**: 176,215 km²
- **Currency**: Uruguayan peso (UYU)
- **Language**: Spanish
- **Time Zone**: UTC-3 (Uruguay Time, UYT)
- **Calling Code**: +598
- **Internet TLD**: .uy
- **President**: Luis Lacalle Pou (2020–2025)
- **Neighboring Countries**: Argentina (west), Brazil (north/east)
- **Key Exports**: Beef, soybeans, cellulose, dairy products, rice
- **Key Imports**: Machinery, chemicals, refined fuel, motor vehicles
- **Major Cities**: Montevideo (1.8M metro), Salto, Ciudad de la Costa, Paysandú

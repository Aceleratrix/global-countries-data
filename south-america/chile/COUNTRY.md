# Chile 🇨🇱
## Overview
- **ISO Code**: CL
- **Capital**: Santiago (Metropolitan Area: ~7.0M population)
- **Population**: 19.5 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 756,096 km²
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.33 trillion (2023, 45th largest economy globally)
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Currency**: Chilean Peso (CLP)
- **Government**: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
- **Current President**: Gabriel Boric (since 2022)
- **Borders**: Peru (north), Bolivia (northeast), Argentina (east), Pacific Ocean (west/south)
- **Time Zone**: UTC-4 (mainland), UTC-6 (Easter Island)

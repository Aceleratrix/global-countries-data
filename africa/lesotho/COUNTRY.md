# Lesotho (🇱🇸)

## Overview
Lesotho, officially the Kingdom of Lesotho, is a landlocked country enclaved within South Africa. It is the 151st largest economy globally with a GDP of $0.05T.

## Key Facts
- **Official Name**: Kingdom of Lesotho
- **Common Name**: Lesotho
- **Capital**: Maseru
- **Population**: 2,116,427 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 30,355 km²
- **GDP**: $0.05T (151st largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Lesotho Loti (LSL), South African Rand (ZAR)
- **Languages**: English (official), Sotho (Sesotho, national)
- **Region**: Africa (Southern Africa)
- **Borders**: South Africa (ZAF)
- **Timezone**: UTC+02:00
- **Gini Index**: 44.9 (2017)
- **Landlocked**: Yes
- **Driving Side**: Left
- **Country Codes**: LS (cca2), LSO (cca3), 426 (ccn3)
- **FIFA Code**: LES
- **Flag Description**: Three horizontal bands of blue, white and green in ratio 3:4:3 with a black mokorotlo (Basotho hat) centered in the white band.

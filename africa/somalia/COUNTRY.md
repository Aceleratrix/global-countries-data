# Somalia 🇸🇴
## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Federal Republic of Somalia
- **Country Code**: SO
- **Capital**: Mogadishu
- **Population**: ~17,600,000 (2023 est)
- **Area**: 637,657 km²
- **Currency**: Somali shilling (SOS)
- **Languages**: Somali (official), Arabic (official)
- **Region**: Africa
- **Subregion**: Eastern Africa
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.06T (142nd largest economy globally)
- **Timezone**: UTC+3
- **Borders**: Ethiopia, Djibouti, Kenya
- **Coastline**: 3,300 km (Indian Ocean, Gulf of Aden)
- **Flag Description**: Light blue field with a white five-pointed star in the center

## Key Facts
- Founding member of the African Union
- Longest coastline in mainland Africa
- Majority ethnic group: Somali (over 85%)
- Major religion: Islam (Sunni, ~99.9%)
- Independence: July 1, 1960 (from Italy and UK)

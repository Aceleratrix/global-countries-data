# Comoros (🇰🇲)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Union of the Comoros
- **Common Name**: Comoros
- **ISO Code**: KM
- **Capital**: Moroni
- **Population**: 850,688 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 1,861 km²
- **GDP**: $0.05 trillion (157th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Comorian franc (KMF)
- **Languages**: Comorian, French, Arabic
- **Government Type**: Federal presidential republic
- **Continent**: Africa
- **Region**: East Africa (Indian Ocean)

## Geography
Comoros is a volcanic archipelago in the Indian Ocean, located between Mozambique and Madagascar. It consists of four main islands: Grande Comore (Ngazidja), Mohéli (Mwali), Anjouan (Ndzuwani), and Mayotte (administered by France but claimed by Comoros).

## Economy
The economy relies on agriculture (vanilla, cloves, ylang-ylang), fishing, and small-scale tourism. GDP is estimated at $0.05T, ranking 157th worldwide.

## Demographics
Population is predominantly of mixed African, Arab, and Malagasy descent. ~98% identify as Sunni Muslim, with small minorities of other faiths.

## Government
Federal presidential republic with power divided between the union government and three island governments (Grande Comore, Mohéli, Anjouan).

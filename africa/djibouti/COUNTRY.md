# Djibouti

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Djibouti
- **Country Codes**: DJ (CCA2), DJI (CCA3)
- **Capital**: Djibouti City
- **Population**: 1,066,809 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 23,200 km²
- **Region**: Africa (Eastern Africa)
- **Official Languages**: Arabic, French
- **Currency**: Djiboutian franc (DJF)
- **Borders**: Eritrea, Ethiopia, Somalia
- **Timezone**: UTC+03:00

## Economic Data
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.05 trillion
- **Global GDP Rank**: 159th
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$4,500 (PPP)

## Key Facts
Strategically located at the entrance to the Red Sea, Djibouti hosts multiple foreign military bases and serves as a critical logistics hub for East Africa and the Middle East.

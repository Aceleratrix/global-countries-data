# Eswatini (Kingdom of Eswatini) 🇸🇿

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Kingdom of Eswatini
- **Common Name**: Eswatini
- **Capital(s)**: Mbabane (administrative), Lobamba (legislative/royal)
- **Country Codes**: SZ (cca2), SWZ (cca3), 748 (ccn3)
- **Region**: Africa (Southern Africa)
- **Population**: 1,160,164 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 17,364 km²
- **Languages**: English (official), Swati (official)
- **Currency**: Swazi lilangeni (SZL, symbol: L)
- **Borders**: Mozambique (MOZ), South Africa (ZAF)
- **Flag**: 🇸🇿

## Economy
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.05 Trillion (2024 estimate)
- **Global GDP Rank**: 150th largest economy
- **Currency**: Swazi lilangeni (pegged 1:1 to South African rand)
- **Major Industries**: Mining (coal, diamonds), agriculture (sugarcane, cotton), manufacturing (textiles)

## Geography
- **Total Area**: 17,364 km²
- **Landlocked**: Yes
- **Climate**: Subtropical, rainy season October–April
- **Terrain**: Mountainous and hilly with lowland plains in the east
- **Highest Point**: Emlembe (1,862m)
- **Lowest Point**: Great Usutu River (21m)

## Government
- **System**: Absolute monarchy
- **Head of State**: King Mswati III (reign started 1986)
- **Constitution**: Enacted 2005
- **Legislature**: Bicameral Parliament (Senate + House of Assembly)

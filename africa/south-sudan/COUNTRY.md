# Republic of South Sudan

## Overview
- **Official Name**: Republic of South Sudan
- **Capital**: Juba
- **Independence**: July 9, 2011 (from Sudan)
- **Area**: 619,745 km²
- **Population**: ~11,088,796 (2023 UN estimate)
- **Currency**: South Sudanese Pound (SSP)
- **Official Language**: English
- **Calling Code**: +211
- **Internet TLD**: .ss
- **ISO Codes**: SS (alpha-2), SSD (alpha-3), 728 (numeric)

## Key Facts
- Youngest sovereign state in Africa, gained independence via 2011 referendum (98.83% voted for secession)
- Landlocked, bordered by Sudan, Ethiopia, Kenya, Uganda, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Central African Republic
- Rich in oil reserves, which account for ~90% of government revenue
- Ongoing political instability since 2013 civil war; Revitalized Peace Agreement signed 2018, transitional government formed 2020
- Home to the Sudd, one of the world's largest wetlands (Ramsar site)

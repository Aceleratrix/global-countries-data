# Zambia (🇿🇲)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Zambia
- **Common Name**: Zambia
- **Capital**: Lusaka
- **Population**: 19,473,125 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 752,612 km²
- **Currency**: Zambian Kwacha (ZMK)
- **Currency Code**: ZMK
- **Languages**: English (official), Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga, Lozi, Kaonde, Lunda
- **Region**: Africa
- **Subregion**: Southern Africa
- **Borders**: Angola, Botswana, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, Tanzania, Zimbabwe
- **GDP**: $0.08T (111th largest economy globally)
- **Independence**: 24 October 1964 (from the United Kingdom)
- **Government Type**: Presidential republic

## Economy
- Primary industries: Copper mining (accounts for ~70% of export earnings), agriculture, tourism, manufacturing
- Key exports: Copper, cobalt, electricity, tobacco, cotton

## Geography
- Landlocked country in Southern Africa, named after the Zambezi River
- Major rivers: Zambezi, Kafue, Luangwa
- Notable landmark: Victoria Falls (on border with Zimbabwe, one of the world's largest waterfalls)

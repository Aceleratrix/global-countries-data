# Cabo Verde (Cape Verde)
## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Republic of Cabo Verde
- **Common Name**: Cabo Verde (Cape Verde)
- **Country Code**: CV (CCA2), CPV (CCA3), 132 (CCN3)
- **Region**: Africa
- **Subregion**: Western Africa
- **Capital**: Praia
- **Population**: ~593,149 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 4,033 km²
- **GDP**: $0.05T (156th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Cape Verdean escudo (CVE)
- **Languages**: Portuguese (official), Kabuverdianu (Creole, national)
- **Timezone**: Atlantic/Cape_Verde
- **Borders**: None (island nation)
- **Independent**: Yes (since 1975, from Portugal)

## Key Facts
- Archipelago of 10 volcanic islands in the central Atlantic Ocean
- Strategic location for transatlantic shipping and aviation
- Service-oriented economy, tourism and maritime services are key sectors
- Stable democracy with regular free elections since 1990s

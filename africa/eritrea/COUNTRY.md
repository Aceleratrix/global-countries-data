# Eritrea

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: State of Eritrea
- **Common Name**: Eritrea
- **Capital**: Asmara
- **Population**: 3,536,765 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 117,600 km²
- **GDP**: $0.05 Trillion (160th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Eritrean nakfa (ERN, symbol Nfk)
- **Languages**: Tigrinya, Arabic, English
- **Geographic Coordinates**: 15.1794° N, 39.7823° E
- **Borders**: Djibouti (DJI), Ethiopia (ETH), Sudan (SUD)
- **Region**: Africa (Eastern Africa)
- **Flag**: 🇪🇷

## Key Facts
- Gained independence from Ethiopia on May 24, 1993.
- Asmara (capital) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site for its Italian colonial architecture.
- Economy relies on agriculture, mining, fisheries, and light manufacturing.
- Official languages: Tigrinya, Arabic, English.

# 🇨🇬 Republic of Congo (República do Congo)
## Basic Info
- ISO 3166-1 alpha-2: CG
- ISO 3166-1 alpha-3: COG
- Numeric Code: 178
- Capital: Brazzaville
- Official Language: French
- Currency: Central African CFA franc (XAF)
- Area: 342,000 km²
- Population (2023): ~6,100,000
- President: Denis Sassou-Nguesso
- Prime Minister: Anatole Collinet Makosso
## Geography
Located in Central Africa, borders Gabon, Cameroon, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of Congo, and Angola's Cabinda exclave. Atlantic Ocean coastline ~170 km.
## Economy
Oil-dependent economy, also forestry, agriculture. GDP (2023): ~$14 billion.

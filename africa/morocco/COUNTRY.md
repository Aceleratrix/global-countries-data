# Morocco 🇲🇦
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: المملكة المغربية (Kingdom of Morocco)
- **Common Name**: Morocco
- **Capital**: Rabat
- **Largest City**: Casablanca
- **Population**: ~37,344,795 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 446,550 km²
- **Currency**: Moroccan dirham (MAD, د.م.)
- **Languages**: Arabic (official), Berber/Amazigh (official), French (widely spoken)
- **GDP**: $0.06T (135th largest economy globally)
- **Region**: Africa (Northern Africa)
- **Borders**: Algeria, Western Sahara, Spain (Ceuta, Melilla)
- **Calling Code**: +212
- **Internet TLD**: .ma

## Key Facts
- Major export: Phosphates, fertilizers, textiles, automotive parts
- Major religions: Islam (official, ~99% Sunni Muslim)
- Government type: Unitary parliamentary semi-constitutional monarchy
- Highest peak: Toubkal (4,167 m) in the Atlas Mountains

# Morocco (ⵍⵎⵖⵔⵉⴱ, المغرب)

## Basic Information
- **Capital**: Rabat
- **Largest City**: Casablanca
- **Population**: 37,384,000 (2024 estimate)
- **Area**: 710,850 km²
- **GDP**: $0.25 trillion (nominal, 2024, 54th largest economy globally)
- **Official Languages**: Arabic, Tamazight (Berber)
- **Currency**: Moroccan dirham (MAD)
- **Government**: Unitary parliamentary semi-constitutional monarchy
- **Current Monarch**: Mohammed VI
- **Current Prime Minister**: Aziz Akhannouch
- **Independence**: March 2, 1956 (from France)
- **Time Zone**: UTC+1 (West Africa Time)
- **Calling Code**: +212
- **Internet TLD**: .ma

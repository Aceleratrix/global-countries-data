# Kenya 🇰🇪
## Overview
- **Economy Rank**: 41st largest globally
- **GDP**: $0.34 trillion (2023 estimate)
- **Population**: ~54 million (2023 estimate)
- **Capital**: Nairobi (population ~5 million)
- **Largest City**: Nairobi
- **Currency**: Kenyan Shilling (KES)
- **Official Languages**: English, Swahili
- **Area**: 580,367 km²
- **President**: William Ruto (since 2022)
- **Time Zone**: East Africa Time (EAT, UTC+3)
- **Calling Code**: +254
- **ISO Code**: KE

## Key Facts
- Major exports: Tea, coffee, horticultural products, textiles
- Member of: African Union, East African Community, Commonwealth of Nations
- Notable landmarks: Maasai Mara National Reserve, Mount Kenya, Amboseli National Park
- Top athletes: Eliud Kipchoge (marathon world record holder), Faith Kipyegon (1500m world record holder)

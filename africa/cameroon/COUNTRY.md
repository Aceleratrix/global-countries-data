# Cameroon (🇨🇲)
## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Republic of Cameroon
- **Common Name**: Cameroon
- **Capital**: Yaoundé
- **Country Code**: CM
- **Region**: Africa
- **Subregion**: Middle Africa

## Key Stats
- **Population**: 27,198,628 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 475,442 km²
- **GDP**: $0.09T (104th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Central African CFA franc (XAF)
- **Languages**: English, French
- **Borders**: Nigeria, Chad, Central African Republic, Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Equatorial Guinea

## Economy
- Global GDP rank: 104th
- GDP value: $0.09 trillion
- Main industries: Oil, agriculture, forestry, mining

## Geography
- Terrain: Coastal plain, central plateau, mountains in west
- Climate: Tropical in south, semi-arid in north
- Major rivers: Sanaga, Benue

## Demographics
- Ethnic groups: Over 250 ethnic groups, including Bamileke, Fulani, Tikar
- Religions: Christianity (70%), Islam (20%), traditional beliefs (10%)

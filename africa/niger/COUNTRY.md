# Niger (🇳🇪 Republic of Niger)
## Overview
- **Common Name**: Niger
- **Official Name**: Republic of Niger
- **Capital**: Niamey (13.52°N, 2.12°E)
- **Population**: 26,312,034 (2023 est.)
- **Area**: 1,267,000 km² (landlocked)
- **GDP**: $0.08T (115th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: West African CFA franc (XOF, Fr)
- **Languages**: French (official)
- **Demonyms**: Nigerien (m/f), Nigérien (m)/Nigérienne (f) in French
- **Borders**: Algeria, Benin, Burkina Faso, Chad, Libya, Mali, Nigeria
- **Timezone**: UTC+01:00
- **Calling Code**: +227
- **ISO Codes**: NE (cca2), NER (cca3), 562 (ccn3)
- **FIFA Code**: NIG
- **Flag Description**: Three equal horizontal bands of orange, white, green with orange circle centered in white band
- **Gini Index**: 34.3 (2014)

## Key Facts
- Independent since 1960 (formerly French Niger)
- Member of the UN, African Union, ECOWAS
- Majority of population practices Islam (over 99%)
- Economy reliant on agriculture, uranium mining, and livestock
- One of the world's poorest countries by GDP per capita
- Home to the Aïr and Ténéré Natural Reserve (UNESCO World Heritage Site)

# Niger (🇳🇪)
## Republic of Niger
- **ISO 3166-1 alpha-2**: NE
- **ISO 3166-1 alpha-3**: NER
- **ISO Numeric**: 562
- **Capital**: Niamey
- **Largest City**: Niamey
- **Population (2024 est.)**: 26,342,000
- **Area**: 1,267,000 km² (7th largest in Africa)
- **Currency**: West African CFA franc (XOF)
- **Official Language**: French
- **Independence**: 3 August 1960 (from France)
- **Government Type**: Semi-presidential republic (de facto military junta since July 2023)
- **Current Head of State**: General Abdourahamane Tiani (Chair of the National Council for the Safeguard of the Homeland)
- **Calling Code**: +227
- **Internet TLD**: .ne
- **Geography**: Landlocked West African country in the Sahel region, bordered by Algeria, Libya, Chad, Nigeria, Benin, Burkina Faso, and Mali
- **Key Facts**: One of the world's largest uranium exporters; 80% of its territory is covered by the Sahara Desert; lowest Human Development Index (HDI) ranking globally (2023-2024 report)

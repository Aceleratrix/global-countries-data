# Guinea (🇬🇳)
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Guinea
- **Common Name**: Guinea
- **Country Code**: GN
- **Capital**: Conakry
- **Region**: Africa
- **Subregion**: Western Africa
- **Population**: 13,531,906 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 245,857 km²
- **GDP (PPP)**: $0.05T (146th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Guinean franc (GNF)
- **Languages**: French (official), Fula, Mandinka, Susu
- **Timezone**: UTC+0

## Quick Facts
- Landlocked: No (Atlantic Ocean coastline)
- Borders: Guinea-Bissau, Senegal, Mali, Côte d'Ivoire, Liberia, Sierra Leone
- Independence: 2 October 1958 (from France)
- Government Type: Unitary semi-presidential republic

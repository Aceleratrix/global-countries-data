# South Africa 🇿🇦

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of South Africa
- **GDP**: $0.32T (2023, World Bank) – 46th largest economy globally
- **Population**: ~60.14 million (2023 estimate)
- **Capital(s)**: Pretoria (administrative), Cape Town (legislative), Bloemfontein (judicial)
- **Largest City**: Johannesburg (~5.8 million metro population)
- **Currency**: South African Rand (ZAR, R)
- **Official Languages**: 11 (Afrikaans, English, isiNdebele, isiXhosa, isiZulu, Sesotho, Setswana, siSwati, Tshivenda, Xitsonga, Sepedi)
- **Area**: 1,221,037 km² (land: 1,214,470 km², water: 6,567 km²)
- **Calling Code**: +27
- **Internet TLD**: .za

## Key Facts
- Known as the "Rainbow Nation" for its diverse cultures and ethnicities.
- Hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup, first African nation to do so.
- Home to Table Mountain (one of the New7Wonders of Nature) and Kruger National Park (one of Africa's largest game reserves).

# Ethiopia 🇪🇹
## Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia

### Quick Facts
- **Capital**: Addis Ababa
- **Population**: 111,652,998 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 1,104,300 km²
- **GDP**: $0.07 trillion (118th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Ethiopian birr (ETB)
- **Official Language**: Amharic
- **Government**: Federal parliamentary republic

### Overview
Ethiopia is a landlocked country in Eastern Africa, the most populous landlocked country in the world. It is the origin of coffee (Coffea arabica) and has a rich history dating back to the Kingdom of Aksum. It is the only African country that successfully resisted European colonization (except for a brief Italian occupation 1935-1941).

# Nigeria 🇳🇬
## Basic Information
- **Country Code**: NG (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2)
- **Capital**: Abuja
- **Largest City**: Lagos (metro population: ~15.3 million, 2023 est)
- **Population**: 223,804,632 (2023 UN estimate, 6th most populous country globally)
- **Total Area**: 923,769 km² (32nd largest country)
- **Currency**: Nigerian Naira (NGN)
- **Official Language**: English
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.48 trillion (28th largest economy globally, 2023 IMF data)
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$2,100 (2023 est)
- **Major Industries**: Crude oil & natural gas extraction, agriculture (cocoa, rubber, palm oil), fintech, Nollywood film production, telecommunications

## Key Facts
- Africa's most populous country, top oil exporter in Africa
- Global leader in fintech (home to 4 unicorns: Flutterwave, Interswitch, Opay, Andela)
- Nollywood is the world's second-largest film industry by volume
- Major Afrobeats music hub (artists like Burna Boy, Wizkid, Davido)

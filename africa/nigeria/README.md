6. politics.yaml - Government and political structure
7. culture.yaml - Cultural and heritage information
8. infrastructure.yaml - Transport, energy, and digital infrastructure
9. education.yaml - Education system and literacy metrics
10. health.yaml - Health outcomes and expenditure
11. trade.yaml - Import/export and trading partner data
12. energy.yaml - Energy production and consumption
13. tech.yaml - Technology and fintech ecosystem data

# Uganda 🇺🇬
## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Republic of Uganda
- **Country Code**: UG
- **Capital**: Kampala
- **Region**: Africa (East Africa)
- **Population**: ~47.2 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 241,550 km²
- **Currency**: Ugandan Shilling (UGX)
- **Languages**: English (official), Swahili (official)
- **Timezone**: UTC+3 (East Africa Time)
- **Borders**: Kenya, South Sudan, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Rwanda, Tanzania

## Economic Profile
- **GDP (2024)**: $0.09 trillion (100th largest economy globally)
- **Main Industries**: Agriculture (coffee, tea, cotton), tourism, manufacturing, mining
- **Key Exports**: Coffee, fish, tobacco, gold

## Key Facts
- Nicknamed "Pearl of Africa"
- Landlocked country with Lake Victoria shoreline
- English and Swahili are official languages
- Member of East African Community (EAC) and African Union (AU)

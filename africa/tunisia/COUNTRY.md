# Tunisia 🇹🇳

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Tunisia
- **Capital**: Tunis (Population: ~2.8 million)
- **Largest City**: Tunis
- **Population**: ~12.2 million (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 163,610 km²
- **Continent**: Africa (North Africa)
- **Currency**: Tunisian Dinar (TND, symbol: د.ت)
- **Official Language**: Arabic
- **Widely Spoken Languages**: French, Berber (Tamazight)
- **Religion**: Sunni Islam (99% of population)
- **Independence**: March 20, 1956 (from France)

## Economy
- **GDP (Nominal)**: $0.13 trillion (2023 IMF estimate)
- **Global GDP Rank**: 79th
- **GDP Per Capita**: ~$11,000 (2023)
- **Major Industries**: Olive oil production (world's 4th largest exporter), dates, textiles, automotive parts, phosphate mining, tourism
- **Unemployment Rate**: ~16% (2023)
- **Inflation Rate**: ~7.5% (2023)

## Key Facts
- Member of Arab League, African Union, Organisation of Islamic Cooperation
- First Arab country to adopt a post-Arab Spring constitution (2014)
- UNESCO World Heritage Sites: Carthage, El Jem Amphitheatre, Dougga, Ichkeul National Park
- Hosted 2004 Africa Cup of Nations, 2024 African Nations Championship (CHAN)

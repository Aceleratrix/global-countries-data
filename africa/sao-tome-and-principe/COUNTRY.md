# Sao Tome and Principe

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Democratic Republic of Sao Tome and Principe
- **Capital**: Sao Tome
- **Population**: 223,368 (2023 UN estimate)
- **Area**: 1,001 km²
- **Official Language**: Portuguese
- **Currency**: Sao Tome and Principe Dobra (STN)
- **Calling Code**: +239
- **Time Zone**: UTC+0
- **ISO Alpha-2**: ST
- **ISO Alpha-3**: STP
- **Independence**: July 12, 1975 (from Portugal)

## Geography
Sao Tome and Principe is an island country in Central Africa, consisting of two main islands: Sao Tome (larger, southern) and Principe (smaller, northern), plus several smaller islets. Located in the Gulf of Guinea.

## Government
- **System**: Semi-presidential republic
- **Current President**: Carlos Vila Nova (since 2021)
- **Current Prime Minister**: Patrice Trovoada (since 2022)

## Key Facts
- Smallest Portuguese-speaking country
- Member of African Union, ECOWAS, CPLP

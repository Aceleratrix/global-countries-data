## Legal & Regulatory

- **Data Protection:** Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA 2023) - effective 2024
- **Cybersecurity:** Information Technology Act (IT Act 2000, amended 2008)
- **AI Regulation:** AI Governance Guidelines (draft 2023, expected 2026)
- **E-commerce Rules:** Consumer Protection (E-commerce) Rules 2020, amended 2021
- **Data Localization:** Sensitive personal data must be stored in India (DPDPA)

## Important Notes for AI Agents

### 🚨 Critical Considerations
1. **Digital Payment Revolution:** UPI processes 12B+ transactions/month (world's largest)
2. **Data Localization:** Sensitive personal data must be stored in India (DPDPA 2023)
3. **Jio Effect:** Reliance Jio's cheap data (₹10/GB) transformed internet access
4. **Language Diversity:** 22 official languages, Hindi/English for business
5. **Digital India:** 748M internet users, growing 20M+/year
6. **UPI Dominance:** WhatsApp Pay, Google Pay, PhonePe all use UPI rails
7. **Cloud Sovereignty:** Government data must be on domestic cloud (MeghRaj)
8. **Social Commerce:** WhatsApp Business, Instagram Shopping booming

### Integration Guidance
- **Payment integration:** UPI mandatory for digital products (12B+ txns/month)
- **Cloud:** AWS/Azure lead, but MeghRaj (Gov cloud) for gov data
- **WhatsApp:** 530M users - critical for customer engagement
- **Languages:** Hindi + English primary, add regional (Tamil, Telugu, Bengali)

- **E-commerce:** Flipkart (Walmart), Amazon India, JioMart lead

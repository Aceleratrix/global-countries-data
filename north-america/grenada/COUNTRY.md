# Grenada (🇬🇩)

## Overview
Grenada is a Caribbean island country in North America, officially known as Grenada. It is the 165th largest economy globally with a GDP of $0.05T.

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: St. George's
- **Population**: 109,021 (2023 est.)
- **Area**: 344 km²
- **Region**: Americas (North America)
- **Subregion**: Caribbean
- **Currency**: Eastern Caribbean dollar (XCD, $)
- **Languages**: English
- **Timezone**: UTC-04:00
- **Driving Side**: Left
- **Calling Code**: +1-473
- **FIFA Code**: GRN
- **ISO Codes**: GD (cca2), GRD (cca3), 308 (ccn3)

## Geography
Grenada is an island nation located in the Caribbean Sea, north of Trinidad and Tobago. It is part of the Lesser Antilles chain.

## Economy
Ranking 165th globally with a GDP of approximately $0.05 trillion, Grenada's economy is primarily driven by tourism, agriculture (notably nutmeg, from which it earns the nickname "Isle of Spice"), and fishing.

## Government
Parliamentary democracy under a constitutional monarchy, with the British monarch as head of state represented by a Governor-General.

## Flag Description
The flag features a red border with three yellow five-pointed stars on top and bottom. The central area has four triangles (yellow top/bottom, green hoist/fly) with a red-centered yellow star and a nutmeg pod on the green hoist triangle.

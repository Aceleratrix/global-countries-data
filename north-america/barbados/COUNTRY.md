# 🇧🇧 Barbados

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Barbados
- **Capital**: Bridgetown
- **Population**: 287,025 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 439 km²
- **Currency**: Barbadian Dollar (BBD)
- **Languages**: English (official), Bajan Creole
- **Government Type**: Parliamentary republic
- **Independence**: November 30, 1966 (from the United Kingdom)

## Geography
Barbados is an island country in the Caribbean region of North America, part of the Lesser Antilles. It is located east of the Caribbean Sea and north of Trinidad and Tobago.

## Economy
Service-based economy, with tourism and offshore banking as key sectors. Agriculture includes sugarcane, vegetables, and cotton.

## Demographics
Majority Afro-Barbadian (over 90%), with small populations of European, Asian, and mixed heritage. Christianity is the dominant religion (mainly Anglican and Pentecostal).

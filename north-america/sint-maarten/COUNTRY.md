# Sint Maarten (🇸🇽)

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Sint Maarten
- **ISO Codes**: SX (alpha-2), SXM (alpha-3)
- **Capital**: Philipsburg
- **Population**: ~42,000 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 34 km²
- **Currency**: Antillian Guilder (ANG)
- **Languages**: Dutch, English
- **Government**: Parliamentary representative democracy under constitutional monarchy
- **Status**: Constituent country of the Kingdom of the Netherlands

## Key Facts
- Occupies the southern 40% of the island of Saint Martin in the Caribbean.
- Gained autonomy in 2010 after the dissolution of the Netherlands Antilles.
- Economy heavily reliant on tourism and international business.
- Home to Princess Juliana International Airport, a major Caribbean aviation hub.

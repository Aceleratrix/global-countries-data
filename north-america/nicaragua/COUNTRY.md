# Nicaragua (República de Nicaragua)
## Overview
Nicaragua is the largest country in Central America, bordered by Honduras to the north, Costa Rica to the south, the Pacific Ocean to the west, and the Caribbean Sea to the east. Its capital and largest city is Managua.

## Key Facts
- **Official Name**: Republic of Nicaragua (República de Nicaragua)
- **Capital**: Managua
- **Largest City**: Managua
- **Area**: 130,375 km²
- **Population (2023)**: ~6,850,000
- **Currency**: Córdoba (NIO)
- **Languages**: Spanish (official), Miskito, Sumo, Rama, Garifuna (indigenous)
- **Demonym**: Nicaraguan
- **Government**: Unitary presidential republic
- **Current President**: Daniel Ortega (since 2007)
- **Vice President**: Rosario Murillo (since 2017)
- **Legislature**: National Assembly

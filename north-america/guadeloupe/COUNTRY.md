# 🇬🇵 Guadeloupe

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Guadeloupe (Overseas Department of France)
- **ISO Code**: GP
- **Capital**: Basse-Terre
- **Largest City**: Pointe-à-Pitre
- **Area**: 1,628 km²
- **Population**: ~395,000 (2023 est.)
- **Currency**: Euro (€, EUR)
- **Time Zone**: UTC-4 (AST)
- **Languages**: French (official), Antillean Creole

## Geography
Guadeloupe is an archipelago in the Caribbean, part of the Leeward Islands. It consists of two main islands: Basse-Terre (volcanic, home to Soufrière volcano) and Grande-Terre (limestone, flat), plus smaller islands like Marie-Galante, La Désirade, and the Îles des Saintes.

## Politics
Overseas department and region of France, fully integrated into the French Republic. Represented in the French National Assembly and Senate. Prefect appointed by French President.

## Economy
Tourism, agriculture (bananas, sugarcane), and light industry. Receives significant financial aid from France.

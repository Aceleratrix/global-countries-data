# 🇻🇬 British Virgin Islands (Ilhas Virgens Britânicas)

## Overview
The British Virgin Islands (BVI) are a British Overseas Territory in the Caribbean, part of the North American region. Capital: Road Town. Population ~30,000. Known for tourism, financial services, and pristine coral reefs.

## Quick Facts
- **ISO Code**: VG
- **Currency**: USD ($)
- **Language**: English (official)
- **Government**: British Overseas Territory (Premier leads local government, Governor represents King Charles III)
- **GDP**: ~$1.5B USD ($50k per capita)

## Key Sectors
- **Tourism**: Major economic driver, including sailing regattas and luxury resorts
- **Financial Services**: Offshore financial center
- **Agriculture**: Small-scale (fruits, vegetables)
- **Fishing**: Commercial and recreational

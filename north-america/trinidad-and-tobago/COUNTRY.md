# 🇹🇹 Trinidad and Tobago

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Trinidad and Tobago
- **Common Name**: Trinidad and Tobago
- **Country Code (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2)**: TT
- **Country Code (ISO 3166-1 alpha-3)**: TTO
- **Capital**: Port of Spain
- **Largest City**: Chaguanas
- **Area**: 5,131 km²
- **Population (2023 Estimate)**: 1,525,663
- **Official Language**: English
- **Currency**: Trinidad and Tobago Dollar (TTD, $)
- **Driving Side**: Left
- **Calling Code**: +1-868
- **Internet TLD**: .tt

## Geography
Trinidad and Tobago is a twin-island country located off the northern edge of South America, 11 km off the coast of Venezuela. Trinidad is the larger island, with Tobago as the smaller island northeast of Trinidad. The country lies in the Caribbean Sea and is part of the West Indies.

## Government
- **System**: Unitary parliamentary republic
- **Head of State**: President (currently Christine Kangaloo as of 2024)
- **Head of Government**: Prime Minister (currently Stuart Young as of 2024)
- **Legislature**: Parliament of Trinidad and Tobago (bicameral: Senate and House of Representatives)

## Economy
Major industries include petroleum and natural gas, petrochemicals, tourism, and agriculture. It is one of the wealthiest countries in the Caribbean by GDP per capita.

## Culture
Known for its Carnival celebration, calypso music, soca, and chutney music. The national sport is cricket, and the country has a diverse population of African, Indian, European, and Chinese descent.

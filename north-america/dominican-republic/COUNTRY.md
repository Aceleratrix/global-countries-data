# Dominican Republic
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Dominican Republic (República Dominicana)
- **Capital**: Santo Domingo
- **Largest City**: Santo Domingo
- **Population**: 11,306,847 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 48,671 km²
- **Currency**: Dominican Peso (DOP, $)
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Government**: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
- **Independence**: February 27, 1844 (from Haiti)
- **Calling Code**: +1-809, +1-829, +1-849
- **Internet TLD**: .do

## Geography
Located on the eastern two-thirds of the island of Hispaniola in the Caribbean, bordered by Haiti to the west, the Atlantic Ocean to the north, and the Caribbean Sea to the south.

## Economy
Key sectors: tourism, mining (gold, silver), agriculture (sugar, coffee, tobacco), manufacturing (textiles, electronics). GDP (2023): ~$113 billion USD.

## Demographics
Ethnic groups: Mixed (European, African, Indigenous) 73%, Black 16%, White 11% (2022 census). Religion: Roman Catholic 57%, Protestant 23%, other 20%.

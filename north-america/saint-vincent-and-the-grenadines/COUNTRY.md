# Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

## Overview
- **Official Name**: Saint Vincent and the Grenadines
- **Common Name**: Saint Vincent and the Grenadines
- **Capital**: Kingstown
- **Population**: 110,940 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 389 km²
- **GDP**: $0.05 trillion (166th largest economy globally)
- **Currency**: Eastern Caribbean Dollar (XCD)
- **Languages**: English
- **Region**: North America (Caribbean)
- **Country Codes**: Alpha-2: VC, Alpha-3: VCT, Numeric: 670

## Key Facts
- Archipelago of 32 islands in the Caribbean Sea
- Gained independence from the United Kingdom on October 27, 1979
- Member of the Commonwealth of Nations, CARICOM, and the United Nations
- Major industries: Tourism, agriculture (bananas, coconuts), rum manufacturing
- Highest point: La Soufrière volcano (1,234 meters)

# 🇨🇦 Canada

**Capital:** Ottawa  
**Population:** 41.3 million (2024)  
**Currency:** Canadian Dollar (CAD, $)  
**Official Languages:** English, French  
**Government:** Federal Parliamentary Democracy under Constitutional Monarchy  
**GDP (Nominal):** $2.1 trillion (2024) – 10th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $52,085  
**HDI:** 0.935 (Very High) – 15th globally  
**Time Zones:** 6 zones (PST, MST, CST, EST, AST, NST)  

## 🌐 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** CA (Alpha-2), CAN (Alpha-3), 124 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .ca
- **Calling Code:** +1
- **USMCA Member:** Yes (with USA & Mexico)
- **G7 Member:** Yes
- **NATO Member:** Yes
- **Commonwealth Realm:** Yes

## 🏢 Business Environment

Canada has a stable, resource-rich economy with strong sectors in natural resources, manufacturing, technology, and financial services. The country is a top destination for AI research and innovation.

**Key Industries:**
- Natural Resources (oil, gas, minerals, timber)
- Automotive Manufacturing (Ontario hub)
- Technology & AI (Toronto-Waterloo, Montreal, Vancouver)
- Financial Services (Big 5 banks)
- Aerospace (Montreal hub)
- Agriculture & Agri-food

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Canada is a global AI leader with the **Pan-Canadian AI Strategy**:
- $2.4 billion invested in AI since 2017
- 3 major AI hubs: Montreal, Toronto-Waterloo, Edmonton
- Home to pioneering AI research (Yoshua Bengio, Geoffrey Hinton, Richard Sutton)
- Vector Institute (Toronto), Mila (Montreal), Amii (Edmonton)

**Major AI Companies/Initiatives:**
- Cohere (Toronto, $2.2B valuation)
- Element AI (acquired by ServiceNow)
- BlackBerry (AI cybersecurity)
- RBC (AI-driven banking)
- Canadian AI startups receive strong government support

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 5.8% (2024)
- **Inflation Rate:** 2.9% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 15% (federal) + provincial (11-16% total)
- **Minimum Wage:** Varies by province ($15-16 CAD/hour)
- **GDP Growth:** 1.8% (2024)

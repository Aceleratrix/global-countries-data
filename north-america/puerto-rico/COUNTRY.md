# Puerto Rico

**Official Name:** Commonwealth of Puerto Rico  
**Capital:** San Juan  
**Population:** 3.2 million (2023 estimate)  
**GDP:** $0.18 trillion (68th largest economy globally)  
**Currency:** United States Dollar (USD)  
**Languages:** Spanish, English  
**Area:** 9,104 km²  
**Continent:** North America  
**Region:** Caribbean  
**Status:** Unincorporated territory of the United States  

## Key Facts
- Archipelago in the Caribbean Sea, part of the Greater Antilles
- San Juan metropolitan area population: ~2 million
- Key economic sectors: Pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, tourism, services
- Adopted US dollar in 1899 after becoming a US territory
- El Yunque National Forest: Only tropical rainforest in the US National Forest system
- Old San Juan: UNESCO World Heritage Site

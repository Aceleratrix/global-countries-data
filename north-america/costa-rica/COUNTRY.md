# Costa Rica 🇨🇷
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Costa Rica
- **GDP**: $0.16T (71st largest economy globally)
- **Population**: ~5.2 million (2023 estimate)
- **Capital**: San José (Metro population: ~1.5 million)
- **ISO Codes**: Alpha-2: CR, Alpha-3: CRI
- **Currency**: Costa Rican colón (CRC)
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Region**: North America
- **Independence**: September 15, 1821 (from Spain)
- **Area**: 51,100 km²
- **HDI**: 0.806 (2021, Very High)

## Key Facts
- Abolished its army in 1949, allocating funds to education and healthcare instead.
- Major exports: Bananas, coffee, medical devices, pineapples.
- Home to 5% of the world's biodiversity despite covering only 0.03% of the Earth's surface.
- First country to meet all criteria for the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in 2015.

# 🇲🇸 Montserrat

## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Montserrat
- **Flag**: 🇲🇸
- **ISO Codes**: MS (alpha-2), MSR (alpha-3)
- **Capital**: Plymouth (de jure, abandoned), Brades (de facto administrative center)
- **Population**: ~5,000 (2023 estimate)
- **Area**: 102 km²
- **GDP (Nominal)**: ~$0.05 billion (2022)
- **Currency**: Eastern Caribbean Dollar (XCD)
- **Official Language**: English
- **Status**: British Overseas Territory

## Geography
Volcanic island in the Leeward Islands (Lesser Antilles), Caribbean Sea. Active Soufrière Hills volcano has caused major eruptions since 1995, abandoning the southern two-thirds of the island.

## Key Facts
- Known as "Emerald Isle of the Caribbean" due to Irish settler heritage and landscape resemblance to Ireland.
- 1995 volcanic eruptions displaced two-thirds of the population, mostly to the UK and Antigua and Barbuda.
- Economy relies on tourism, small-scale agriculture, and construction.

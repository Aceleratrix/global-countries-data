# Panama 🇵🇦
## Basic Information
- **Official Name**: Republic of Panama
- **Capital**: Panama City (population: ~2,000,000)
- **Largest City**: Panama City
- **Population**: ~4,400,000 (2025 est.)
- **Area**: 75,417 km²
- **GDP**: $0.16 trillion (72nd largest economy globally, 2024 est.)
- **Currency**: Panamanian Balboa (pegged 1:1 to USD); USD also legal tender
- **Official Language**: Spanish
- **Government**: Unitary presidential constitutional republic
- **Current President**: José Raúl Mulino (elected 2024)
- **Independence**: November 3, 1903 (from Colombia)

## Key Facts
- Home to the Panama Canal, a critical global shipping chokepoint handling ~5% of world trade
- Dollarized economy with no central bank
- Major regional banking and logistics hub
- Biodiversity hotspot with 30% of territory protected

# Saint Lucia 🇱🇨
## Basic Info
- **Official Name**: Saint Lucia
- **Country Code (ISO 3166-1 alpha-2)**: LC
- **Capital**: Castries
- **Region**: North America (Caribbean)
- **Subregion**: Caribbean
- **Population**: ~180,000 (2023 est.)
- **Area**: 617 km²
- **Currency**: Eastern Caribbean Dollar (XCD)
- **Languages**: English (official), Saint Lucian Creole French
- **Borders**: None (island nation)
- **GDP**: $0.05T (164th largest economy globally)
## Key Facts
- Independent from UK: February 22, 1979
- Head of State: King Charles III (represented by Governor-General)
- Head of Government: Prime Minister
- Major Industries: Tourism, banana export, light manufacturing
- Top Export Partners: US, UK, CARICOM states

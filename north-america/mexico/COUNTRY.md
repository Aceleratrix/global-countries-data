# 🇲🇽 Mexico

**Capital:** Mexico City  
**Population:** 129.5 million (2024)  
**Currency:** Mexican Peso (MXN, $)  
**Official Language:** Spanish  
**Government:** Federal Presidential Republic  
**GDP (Nominal):** $1.3 trillion (2024) – 15th largest economy globally  
**GDP Per Capita:** $10,400  
**HDI:** 0.781 (High) – 86th globally  
**Time Zone:** UTC-8 to UTC-5 (4 time zones)  

## 🌎 Quick Facts

- **ISO 3166-1:** MX (Alpha-2), MEX (Alpha-3), 484 (Numeric)
- **Internet TLD:** .mx
- **Calling Code:** +52
- **USMCA Member:** Yes (with USA & Canada)
- **OECD Member:** Yes
- **G20 Member:** Yes

## 🏢 Business Environment

Mexico is a major manufacturing hub and the 2nd largest economy in Latin America. Strong sectors in automotive, aerospace, electronics, and tourism.

**Key Industries:**
- Automotive Manufacturing (6th largest producer globally)
- Aerospace (fastest growing sector)
- Electronics & Medical Devices
- Oil & Gas (Pemex)
- Tourism (Cancun, Riviera Maya, Mexico City)
- Agriculture (avocado #1 exporter globally)
- Tech Services (nearshoring boom)

## 🤖 AI Readiness

Mexico is developing its AI ecosystem with focus on:
- **National AI Strategy** (in development)
- Growing nearshoring trend bringing tech investments
- Strong talent pool (1M+ engineering graduates)
- Tech hubs: Mexico City, Guadalajara ("Silicon Valley of Mexico"), Monterrey

**Major Tech/AI Initiatives:**
- **Kavak** – AI-driven used car platform ($8.7B valuation)
- **Bitso** – Latin America's largest crypto exchange
- **Clara** – AI-powered fintech for SMEs
- **Rappi** – AI delivery optimization (HQ in Colombia, major in Mexico)

## 📊 Economic Highlights

- **Unemployment Rate:** 2.8% (2024, historic low)
- **Inflation Rate:** 4.6% (2024)
- **Corporate Tax Rate:** 30%
- **Minimum Wage:** MXN$248.93/day (2024, ~US$14.60)
- **GDP Growth:** 2.1% (2024)

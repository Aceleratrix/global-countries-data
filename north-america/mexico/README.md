Mexico's **National AI Strategy** (in development) positions it as a nearshoring hub:
- Growing tech talent pool (1M+ engineering graduates)
- Tech hubs: Mexico City, Guadalajara ("Silicon Valley of Mexico"), Monterrey
- Nearshoring boom bringing AI investments from USA/Asia

- **Young Population:** Median age 29 years (vs 38+ in USA/China)

- **Digital Payments:** OXXO cash network, CoDi (central bank digital)
